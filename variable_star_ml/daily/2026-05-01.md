**English Title**:  
Six New Variable Stars Discovered from Ground-Based Photometry and Characterized with TESS Data

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.27176v1 公告类型：新稿
摘要：我们报告了六颗新变星的发现。这些目标是作者使用小型望远镜和 CMOS 相机观测若干天区后，通过探索性分析识别出来的。搜索过程中，使用 AstroImageJ 对数百个目标进行了同步测光，并结合作者开发的基于 Python 的自定义工具来生成测光孔径列表，并在整个视场范围内可视化光变曲线。随后，我们利用 TESS 数据进一步刻画了这些变星的性质，并给出了其相关结果。文中还描述了数据分析流程，包括作者开发的软件包 LCV 和 VS-fit，它们分别用于周期图分析和光变曲线建模。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.27176v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: We report the discovery of six new variable stars identified through an exploratory analysis of several sky fields observed by the author using a small telescope and a CMOS camera. The search employed simultaneous photometry of hundreds of objects with AstroImageJ, supplemented by custom Python-based tools developed by the author to generate aperture lists and visualize light curves across the full field of view. The variables were further characterized using TESS data, and their properties are presented. We also describe the data analysis workflow, including the software packages LCV and VS-fit, developed by the author for periodogram analysis and light-curve modelling.

## Paper 2

**Chinese Title**:  
脉动驱动的氦输运作为布拉日科效应的潜在来源

**English Title**:  
Pulsation-driven helium transport as a potential source of the Blazhko effect

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.27925v1 公告类型：新稿
摘要：我们提出了一个高度简化的非线性流体动力学模型，用以模拟天琴座 RR 型变星中振幅调制（通常称为布拉日科效应）的主要观测特征。该模型基于如下假设：脉动所产生的周期性流动会携带 He I 和 He II 电离区中的过量氦。一旦这些额外氦积累到某个临界值，便会触发一种瑞利-泰勒型不稳定性，导致过量氦发生回流；而由于脉动持续将氦向上抽运，这一过程会周而复始。氦含量的这种周期性变化会导致氦电离区对辐射通量阻滞效率的变化，并表现为脉动振幅的长期周期性变化。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.27925v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: We present a highly simplified nonlinear hydrodynamical model to emulate the main observed features of amplitude modulation (commonly known as Blazhko effect) in RR Lyrae stars. The model is based on the assumption that the periodic flow generated by the pulsation carries surplus helium in the ionization zones He I and II. Once this extra helium reaches a critical amount, a Rayleigh-Taylor-type instability leads to a back-flow of the surplus helium and the process starts over again, due to the continuing effect of pumping helium upward by the pulsation. This periodic variation of helium leads to various efficiency of radiation flux blocking in the helium ionization zone that shows up as a long-term periodic variation of the pulsation amplitude.

## Paper 3

**Chinese Title**:  
A型超巨星食双星 BM Cas：一个演化后的中等质量系统

**English Title**:  
The A Supergiant Eclipsing Binary BM Cas: An Evolved, Intermediate Mass System

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.27109v1 公告类型：新稿
摘要：本文利用覆盖五个轨道周期的光谱，研究了周期为198天的食双星 BM Cas 的演化状态。由 SiII 6347 和 SiII 6371 测得的径向速度追踪了主星的运动，得到的质量函数与 Popper（1977）基于 MgII 4481 得到的结果相近。环系统壳层的吸收使得利用 FeII 线测量恒星速度变得复杂。与该壳层相关的许多 Halpha 发射与吸收特征都与主星运动同步变化，因此我们提出该壳层可能由经 L2 点流出系统的物质形成。红外光谱能量分布仅在波长大于 5 微米时才偏离 A 型超巨星的典型分布，并考察了次星被不透明包层遮蔽的模型。将档案中的 V 波段测光与模型光变曲线比较后，我们认为这颗 A 型超巨星已接近充满、或者正在充满其洛希瓣，而迄今尚未探测到的次星可能比主星质量更大。基于 BM Cas 及其环境的整体性质，我们推测它是一个大陵五型或后大陵五型系统，其中这颗 A 型超巨星原本是质量更大的成员。若确实如此，则 BM Cas 中恒星的初始质量应处于中等质量范围。主星的一些测光特征还表明其存在天鹅座α型变异。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.27109v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: The evolutionary state of the 198 day eclipsing binary BM Cas is examined using spectra that cover five orbital cycles. Radial velocities measured from SiII 6347 and SiII 6371 track the motion of the primary, and a mass function is found that is similar to that obtained by Popper(1977) from MgII 4481. Absorption from a circumsystem shell complicates efforts to measure stellar velocities from FeII lines. Many of the characteristics of Halpha emission and absorption that are associated with the shell vary in sync with the motion of the primary, and it is suggested that the shell may form from material that exits the system from L2. The infrared spectral-energy distribution departs from that of an A supergiant only at wavelengths > 5um, and models are examined in which the secondary is obscured by an opaque envelope. Archived V band photometry is compared with model light curves, and it is concluded that the A supergiant is close to filling, or is filling, its Roche lobe, and that the as-yet undetected secondary may be more massive than the primary. Based on the overall properties of BM Cas and its environment, we suggest that it is an Algol or post-Algol system, in which the A supergiant was originally the more massive component. If this is the case then the stars in BM Cas had intermediate initial masses. Some of the photometric characteristics of the primary are indicative of alpha Cyg-type variability.

## Paper 4

**Chinese Title**:  
方位角发电机波导致的近双星食时变化

**English Title**:  
Eclipsing time variations in close binaries produced by azimuthal dynamo waves

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.27609v1 公告类型：新稿
摘要：共同包层后双星（PCEBs）中的食时变化（ETV）的本质至今仍不清楚。环双星行星的解释往往经不起时间检验，而 Applegate 机制又受到能量学约束，并且难以重现观测结果。
基于近期关于磁致 ETV 的解析模型和恒星发电机模拟，我们旨在用沿恒星方位角方向漂移的非轴对称磁场，即所谓的方位角发电机波（ADW），来解释 ETV。
我们在一个双星系统中引入了一个随时间变化的非轴对称四极矩（$Q$）。随后求解系统动力学，计算由此产生的食发生时刻，并构建 O-C 图。我们对不同的 $Q$ 振幅、周期、恒星质量以及双星间距进行了多组模拟。
ADW 会自然地产生与观测相似的 O-C 图特征形状。取决于 $Q$ 变化的快慢，解可以表现为 O-C 的陡降，并产生类似 QS Vir 中观测到的振幅；也可以表现为类似 V471 Tau 或 NN Ser 中的类正弦形态。我们还发现，食时变化的振幅可从几十秒到数百秒不等。
ADW 为 ETV 提供了一种自洽的解释，因为它们是发电机理论中的预期现象。它们能够解释观测到的 O-C 图中的多种特征。正如发电机模拟所表明的，ADW 在快速自转恒星中很容易被激发，这缓解了 Applegate 机制所要求的能量约束。它们产生非轴对称的 $Q$，进而导致 ETV，从而可以解释 O-C 图的长期变化。我们预期在这种情况下，所得 O-C 图并非严格周期性的；这不同于第三天体解释，因为后者通常意味着严格周期性，除非再引入额外机制。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.27609v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: The nature of eclipsing time variations (ETVs) in post-common-envelope binaries (PCEBs) is still unknown. Circumbinary planets routinely fail the test of time and the Applegate mechanism has energetic constraints and problems in reproducing observations.
Based on recent analytic models of magnetically-induced ETVs and stellar dynamo simulations, we aim at explaining ETVs via non-axisymmetric magnetic fields that drift in the azimuthal direction of the star, know as azimuthal dynamo waves (ADWs).
We implement a time-varying non-axisymmetric quadrupole moment ($Q$) in a binary system. We solve for the dynamics of the system, compute the resulting eclipsing times, and construct O-C, diagrams. We perform several simulations with different amplitudes of $Q$, periods, stellar masses and binary separations.
ADWs naturally give rise to characteristic shapes in the O-C diagram that resemble observations. Depending on how fast $Q$ changes, the solutions can have a sharp decrease in O-C producing amplitudes such as the one obtained in QS Vir, or sinusoidal-like shapes such as in V471 Tau or NN Ser. We also find that the amplitude of the eclipsing times varies from tens to hundreds of seconds.
ADWs offer a self-consistent explanation for ETVs as they are expected in dynamo theory. They can explain a variety of features in the observed O-C diagrams. As suggested by dynamo simulations, ADWs are easily excited in rapidly rotating stars, alleviating energetic constrains required in the context of the Applegate mechanism. They produce non-axis $Q$ that in turn produce ETVs that can account for the long-term variation of the O-C, diagrams. We expect in this case that the resulting O-C diagrams are not strictly periodic, unlike explanations based on a third body that would imply a strict periodicity unless additional mechanisms are being invoked.
