**Chinese Title**:  
PLATO 首个长期指向视场 LOPS2 中 TESS 目标的变异分类：公开变异星表第一版

**English Title**:  
Variability classification of TESS targets in LOPS2, the first long-term pointing field of PLATO. Version 1 of the public variability catalogue

**Chinese Abstract**:  
PLAnetary Transits and Oscillations of stars（PLATO）任务预计将于 2027 年 1 月发射。其总数据率中的 8\% 将用于经批准的 Guest Observer 提案所选定的补充科学目标。我们的目标是为 PLATO 首个长期观测视场 LOPS2 提供一个开源的变异星星表。为此，我们利用了凌日系外行星巡天卫星（TESS）的现有观测数据，TESS 已观测到 LOPS2 中的大量恒星。我们对来自 TESS-Gaia Light Curve pipeline（TGLC，$G\lesssim17$）的 3800 万条校准孔径光变曲线进行了分类，这些数据对应于 LOPS2 中 600 万个独立源。分类采用了两套机器学习框架，即深度神经网络和基于特征的梯度提升决策树集成模型。我们结合两者的预测结果，构建了 LOPS2 变异星表的第一版；同时对其中一个已分类光变曲线子样本进行了人工核验，并对整体结果进行了统计分析，以验证方法学并评估星表中恒星的变异性质及参数。我们的分类结果表明，约 72% 的光变曲线主要受仪器或数据处理流程引入的信号主导，其余 28% 对应 360 万颗独立的候选变异星，包括脉动星、自转变星和食双星。候选脉动星在频率、振幅、自转以及基本参数方面表现出多样行为。为保证样本纯度，对颜色、光度、主导频率及其振幅以及近邻天体存在情况进行筛选是有帮助的。我们向学界发布了这一版 PLATO LOPS2 变异星表，供进一步研究和检验。就自动化分类流程生成的变异星表而言，它是迄今规模最大的星表之一。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.12758v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: The PLAnetary Transits and Oscillations of stars (PLATO) mission is expected to launch in January 2027. A total of 8\% of its data rate will be dedicated to complementary science targets selected from approved Guest Observer proposals. We seek to provide an open-source catalogue of variable stars in PLATO's first long-term observing field, LOPS2. We want to use existing observations from the Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite (TESS), which has observed many stars in LOPS2. We classified 38 million calibrated aperture light curves from the TESS-Gaia Light Curve pipeline (TGLC, $G\lesssim17$) for 6 million unique sources in LOPS2 with two machine learning frameworks -- a deep neural network and a feature-based gradient-boosted decision-tree ensemble. We combined their predictions to create this first version of the LOPS2 variability catalogue, performed manual vetting of a sub-sample classified light curves, and a statistical analysis of the results to validate our methodology and to assess the variability properties and parameters of the stars in the catalogue. Our classification resulted in the identification of approximately 72% of the light curves having dominant instrument- or pipeline-induced signal, with the remaining 28% representing 3.6 million individual candidate variable stars, including pulsating, rotating, and eclipsing stars. Candidate pulsators exhibit varied behaviour in terms of their frequencies, amplitudes, rotation, and fundamental parameters. To ensure purity of the samples, filtering on colour, luminosity, the dominant frequency and its amplitude, and presence of close neighbours is helpful. We provide the first version of our PLATO LOPS2 variability catalogue to the community for further study and scrutiny. It is to date one of the largest catalogues of variable stars from an automated classification pipeline.

**Chinese Title**:  
KIC 3868420：一颗正穿越赫罗图空隙的高振幅 $\delta$ Scuti-$\gamma$ Dor 混合型恒星

**English Title**:  
KIC 3868420: A high-amplitude $\delta$ Scuti-$\gamma$ Dor hybrid star crossing the Hertzsprung gap

**Chinese Abstract**:  
我们报告了对 KIC 3868420 的光度学和星震学分析。这是一颗新识别出的高振幅 $\delta$ Scuti-$\gamma$ Doradus 混合型恒星，位于赫罗图空隙，这是一段寿命很短且极少被观测到的后主序演化阶段。利用四年的 Kepler 长时标采样光度数据，我们探测到 36 个显著频率，其中包括 11 个独立模，覆盖低频和高频两个频段。采用包含自转效应的 MESA 和 GYRE 进行网格建模表明，其中 5 个独立频率可由径向 p 模与非径向 g 模的组合来拟合，支持其混合型本质。最佳拟合模型给出该星为一颗已演化的后主序恒星（$M \sim 2.26 - 2.30 M_\odot$, $R \sim 4.41 - 4.43 R_\odot$, $\tau \sim 5.4 \times 10^{8}$ yr），但由于自转和未知倾角带来的简并性，尚无法得到唯一解。因此，KIC 3868420 是赫罗图空隙中高振幅混合型脉动星的一个罕见实例，表明在一颗快速演化的恒星中，高振幅脉动可以与混合的 p/g 模行为共存。这一发现凸显了空间光度观测在识别此类稀有天体方面的价值。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.12399v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: We report a photometric and asteroseismic analysis of KIC 3868420, a newly identified high-amplitude $\delta$ Scuti-$\gamma$ Doradus hybrid star located in the Hertzsprung gap - a short-lived and rarely observed post-main-sequence phase. Using four years of Kepler long-cadence photometry, we detect 36 significant frequencies, including 11 independent modes spanning both low- and high-frequency regimes. Grid-based modeling with MESA and GYRE, including rotation, shows that five independent frequencies match a combination of radial p-modes and non-radial g-modes, supporting its hybrid nature. The best-fit models yield an evolved post-main-sequence star ($M \sim 2.26 - 2.30 M_\odot$, $R \sim 4.41 - 4.43 R_\odot$, $\tau \sim 5.4 \times 10^{8}$ yr), although degeneracies from rotation and unknown inclination preclude a unique solution. KIC 3868420 thus represents a rare example of a high-amplitude hybrid pulsator in the Hertzsprung gap, demonstrating that high-amplitude pulsation can coexist with mixed p/g-mode behavior in a rapidly evolving star. This finding highlights the value of space-based photometry for identifying such rare objects.

**Chinese Title**:  
利用 Gaia 检验完全和部分剥离低质量恒星模型：对热亚矮星、双星 RR Lyrae 以及黑洞伪装者的启示

**English Title**:  
Testing models for fully and partially stripped low-mass stars with Gaia: Implications for hot subdwarfs, binary RR Lyrae, and black hole impostors

**Chinese Abstract**:  
当低质量（$\lesssim 2$ $M_{\odot}$）红巨星在氦闪之前不久因伴星作用而失去其包层时，随之发生的质量转移可形成包含热亚矮星、水平分支星和低质量红团块星的双星系统。近期研究预测，这类产物构成一个连续谱，从完全剥离形成的热亚矮星，到部分剥离形成的水平分支星和红团块星；并提出如果部分剥离后留下的一颗正在燃烧氦的恒星位于不稳定带内，则可形成年轻、富金属的 RR Lyrae 星。为了能够与观测直接比较，我们在一个模拟的、类似银河系的星系中建立这些双星模型，采用现实的、依赖金属丰度的恒星形成历史和三维尘埃图；再利用 Gaia 的扫描律生成历元天体测量数据，并用 Gaia DR3 中采用的级联天体测量模型进行拟合。我们将模拟族群与 DR3 中关于热亚矮星、RR Lyrae 星以及具有高天体测量质量函数的红巨星观测进行比较。模型显著高估了具有天体测量双星解的热亚矮星数量，部分原因在于预测的通量比比观测结果更不均衡。模型还预测应有 $\gtrsim 100$ 颗 RR Lyrae 星具有 DR3 天体测量轨道解，但实际并未观测到任何此类天体。我们据此认为，天文单位尺度双星中的 RR Lyrae 星可能比预测的要稀少得多。相比之下，该模型可以较合理地解释具有高天体测量质量函数的红团块星族群，我们将其解释为潜在的黑洞伪装者。我们预测，在对更长周期更敏感的 DR4 中，可探测到的剥离恒星双星将增加约 $\sim 10 \times$，这将对宽轨道系统给出更严格的检验。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2602.21289v2 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: When low-mass ($\lesssim 2$ $M_{\odot}$) red giants lose their envelopes to a companion just before the helium flash, the resulting mass transfer can produce binaries hosting hot subdwarfs, horizontal branch stars, and undermassive red clump stars. Recent work predicts a continuum of such products, from fully stripped hot subdwarfs to partially stripped horizontal branch and red clump stars, and suggests that young, metal-rich RR Lyrae can form when partial stripping leaves a helium-burning star in the instability strip. To enable direct comparison with observations, we model these binaries in a simulated Milky Way-like galaxy with a realistic metallicity-dependent star formation history and 3D dust map, generate epoch astrometry using Gaia's scanning law, and fit it with the cascade of astrometric models applied in Gaia DR3. We compare the simulated population to DR3 observations of hot subdwarfs, RR Lyrae, and red giants with high astrometric mass functions. The model significantly overpredicts the number of hot subdwarfs with astrometric binary solutions, partly because the predicted flux ratios are more unequal than observed. It also predicts $\gtrsim 100$ RR Lyrae with DR3 astrometric orbital solutions, while none are observed. We conclude that RR Lyrae in au-scale binaries may be substantially rarer than predicted. In contrast, the model plausibly explains the population of red clump stars with high astrometric mass functions, which we interpret as potential black hole impostors. We predict that $\sim 10 \times$ more stripped-star binaries will be detectable in DR4, whose sensitivity to longer periods will more strongly test wide-orbit systems.
