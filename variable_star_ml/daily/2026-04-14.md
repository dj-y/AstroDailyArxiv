## Paper 1

**Chinese Title**:  
银河系恒星流中 RR Lyrae 星的系统普查

**English Title**:  
Systematic census of RR Lyrae stars in Milky Way stellar streams

**Chinese Abstract**:  
背景。目前银河系中已知近 150 条潮汐流，但只有少数拥有完整的相空间信息。RR Lyrae 星（RRL）作为标准烛光，为探测这些结构提供了强有力的方法，然而迄今仅在不到十条恒星流中识别出这类恒星。目标。我们研究 galstreams 库中所有已报告自行的已知恒星流中的 RRL 种群，开展首次针对这类恒星的系统性普查。我们的目标是识别可能的 RRL 成员，描绘沿恒星流的距离分布，并比较恒星流及其前身系统中的 RRL 种群。方法。我们使用目前最大的 RRL 星表并集（Gaia DR3 SOS、PS1 和 ASAS-SN-II），构建贝叶斯概率成员模型，并在所研究的 56 条恒星流中找到 361 颗 RRL。结果。i）我们发现，在 56 条恒星流中有 32 条的潮汐尾中存在 RRL，其中 13 条有已知前身系统，19 条没有；其中 13 条的尾部包含超过 3 颗 RRL。ii）我们在其中 31 条恒星流中报告了新的 RRL 探测结果，从而锚定了距离，特别是为其中 5 条推断出新的距离梯度。iii）我们的方法为每条轨迹给出了距离和自行的内禀弥散估计，并对预期污染进行了统计量化。iv）这次普查揭示了一些复杂的起源历史，例如我们为 M92 提出的一个包含多个前身系统的新的可能起源情景。v）我们发现，潮汐尾中是否存在 RRL 与前身系统解体的晚期阶段有关。结论。这项普查是识别当前已报告轨迹下哪些恒星流含有显著数量 RRL 的第一步，同时也提供了一份均一且稳健的 RRL 成员星表，并附有精确的经验距离；这对于全面分析这些结构的相空间，以及将其作为研究银河系历史和引力势的探针，至关重要。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.10844v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Context. Nearly 150 tidal streams are known in the Milky Way, but full phase-space information exists for only a few. RR Lyrae stars (RRL), as standard candles, provide a powerful way to probe these structures, yet they have been identified in less than a dozen streams. Aims. We study the RRL population in all known stellar streams with reported proper motions in the galstreams library, performing the first systematic census of these stars. Our goals are to identify likely RRL members, map distances along streams, and compare RRL populations in streams and their progenitors. Method. We use a union of the largest RRL catalogs (Gaia DR3 SOS, PS1, and ASAS-SN-II) to construct a Bayesian probabilistic membership model and find 361 RRL in the 56 streams studied. Results. i) We find that 32 of the 56 streams have RRL in their tidal tails -- 13 with progenitors and 19 without; 13 of these contain more than 3 RRL in their tails. ii) We report new RRL detections in 31 of these streams, anchoring distances and, in particular, inferring new distance gradients for 5 of them. iii) Our method provides intrinsic dispersion estimates in distance and proper motion for each track and statistically quantifies the expected contamination. iv) The census revealed some complex origin histories, such as the new plausible origin scenario we propose for M92 with multiple progenitors. v) We find that the presence of RRL in the tidal tails is linked to the late stages of progenitor dissolution. Conclusions. This census represents a first step toward identifying which of the studied stellar streams contain a significant number of RRL based on currently reported tracks while also providing a homogeneous and robust catalog of RRL members with precise empirical distances, crucial for a full phase-space analysis of these structures and their use as probes of the Galaxy's history and gravitational potential.

Bruno Dom\'inguez, Cecilia Mateu, Pau Ramos, Guillaume F. Thomas

## Paper 2

**Chinese Title**:  
天猫座 BE：位于最高偏心率双星系统中的一颗脉动星，其伴星为大质量不可见天体

**English Title**:  
BE Lyncis: A Pulsating Star in the Most Eccentric Binary with a Massive Unseen Companion

**Chinese Abstract**:  
我们报告发现了一个偏心率异常高的双星系统天猫座 BE（BE~Lyn），其伴星质量可能达到 $\gtrsim 2.5~M_{\odot}$，并有望是一颗致密天体。通过结合 TESS 测光数据与跨越 39 年的大量极大光时刻数据，我们确认 BE~Lyn 是一颗高振幅 $\delta$ Scuti 星，处于一个轨道周期约为 $\approx15.9$ 年、偏心率高达 $e=0.9989^{+0.0008}_{-0.0021}$（95% 置信水平下大于 0.9968）的双星系统中。这是迄今在所有双星系统中被可靠测得的最极端偏心率。动力学约束将轨道倾角限制在 $i \lesssim 10.1^{\circ}$，这意味着伴星质量 $M_2 \gtrsim 2.5~M_{\odot}$，从而表明该伴星是一颗致密天体。该质量意味着它最有可能是一颗黑洞；若它反而是一颗快速自转的中子星，那么它将是目前已知质量最大的中子星。若黑洞解释成立，它将成为距离地球最近的此类天体。该系统为研究强引力场中的星震学以及超高偏心率双星的形成与演化提供了独特实验室。我们的工作展示了如何利用脉动星中的光行时效应揭示一个致密伴星，为在非相互作用双星中探测黑洞提供了一种新方法。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2601.12999v3 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: We report the discovery of an exceptionally eccentric binary system, BE Lyncis (BE~Lyn), which might host a compact companion with mass $\gtrsim 2.5~M_{\odot}$. By combining TESS photometry with an extensive set of times of maximum light spanning 39~years, we identify BE~Lyn as a high-amplitude $\delta$ Scuti star in a binary with an orbital period of $\approx15.9$~years and an extraordinary eccentricity of $e=0.9989^{+0.0008}_{-0.0021}$ ($>0.9968$ at 95% confidence) -- the most extreme eccentricity reliably measured for any binary system. Dynamical constraints limit the orbital inclination to $i \lesssim 10.1^{\circ}$, implying a companion mass $M_2 \gtrsim 2.5~M_{\odot}$, which identifies the companion as a compact object. This mass points to it most likely being a black hole; if instead it is a rapidly rotating neutron star, it would be the most massive known. If the black hole interpretation holds, it would be the closest such object to Earth. This system provides a unique laboratory for studying asteroseismology in strong gravitational fields, as well as the formation and evolution of extremely eccentric binaries. Our work demonstrates the use of the light-travel time effect in a pulsating star to reveal a compact companion, offering a novel method for detecting black holes in non-interacting binaries.

**Authors**:  
Jia-Shu Niu, Ying Zhang, Hui-Fang Xue

## Paper 3

**Chinese Title**:  
两颗新磁性灾变变星的 TESS 光变曲线：一颗位于最短周期附近的不同步偏振子，以及一颗自转与轨道周期比很大的食双星系统

**English Title**:  
TESS light curves of two new magnetic cataclysmic variables: an asynchronous polar at the period minimum, and an eclipsing system with a large spin-to-orbit ratio

**Chinese Abstract**:  
近年来，磁性灾变变星（mCV）研究中的一个新进展是发现了一类轨道周期小于 2 小时、且白矮星自转周期与轨道周期之比明显高于长周期同类天体的不同步自转 mCV。我们报告在这一类别中又发现了两个 mCV。第一个是 Gaia21akb，它是在最短周期附近的一颗候选不同步偏振子。虽然仅凭 TESS 测光还无法最终确定其轨道周期，但其最可能的轨道周期为 1.29 小时；若成立，这将使其成为已知偏振子中轨道周期第二短的系统，并对应自转/轨道周期比 0.9879。本文研究的第二个系统是 ZTF18aazmehw，这是一颗食型 mCV，轨道周期为 1.50 小时，自转/轨道周期比为 0.867。与不同步偏振子的通常预期相反，ZTF18aazmehw 没有显示出可辨识的磁极切换证据，并且可能具有类似吸积盘的结构。具有较大自转/轨道周期比的短周期不同步 mCV 数量不断增加，这增强了理论预言的可信度：当白矮星磁场足够强且轨道间距收缩到足够小时，不同步自转的 mCV 可以重新实现同步。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2603.18143v2 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: A recent development in the study of magnetic cataclysmic variable stars (mCVs) has been the identification of asynchronously spinning mCVs with orbital periods <2 h that have significantly higher white dwarf spin-to-orbital period ratios than their longer-period counterparts. We report the discovery of two additional mCVs in this class. The first, Gaia21akb, is a candidate asynchronous polar at the period minimum. While TESS photometry cannot, in isolation, lead to a conclusive identification of the orbital period, the probable orbital period of 1.29 h would be the second-shortest of any known polar and would result in a spin-to-orbit ratio of 0.9879. The second system in our study, ZTF18aazmehw, is an eclipsing mCV with a 1.50 h orbital period and a spin-to-orbit ratio of 0.867. Contrary to expectations for an asynchronous polar, ZTF18aazmehw does not show discernible evidence of pole switching and might possess a disk-like structure. The increasing number of short-period asynchronous mCVs with large spin-to-orbit ratios lends credence to theoretical predictions that asynchronously rotating mCVs with sufficiently strong white dwarf magnetic fields can achieve synchronization when their orbital separations have shrunk sufficiently.

Colin Littlefield, Krystian Ilkiewicz, Paul A. Mason, Peter Garnavich, Simone Scaringi

## Paper 4

**Chinese Title**:  
基于周期—光度图测定渐近巨星支恒星的年龄和质量

**English Title**:  
Ages and masses of asymptotic giant branch stars from the period--luminosity diagram

**Chinese Abstract**:  
本文展示了一种测定质量介于 0.8 至约 6 M$_\odot$ 的渐近巨星支（AGB）恒星年龄和质量的方法，其基础是将恒星在周期—绝对星等图中的位置与理论演化模型进行比较。对于银河系恒星样本，该方法给出的误差（分别为统计误差和系统误差）约为：年龄 $^{+29}_{-35} \pm 15$%，初始质量 $^{+14}_{-7} \pm 7$%，当前质量 $^{+17}_{-11} \pm 27$%。不过，该方法对单颗恒星的适用性强烈依赖于其在 $P-L$ 图中的位置以及该位置的不确定性。本文将该方法应用于已发表的来自 \emph{Gaia}、NESS、DEATHSTAR 和 ATOMIUM 巡天的 AGB 恒星样本。将这些巡天的统计样本与恒星演化模型的预期进行比较后发现，大多数 AGB 样本都偏向于年龄更年轻、质量更高的恒星。我们得到银河系 AGB 恒星的平均质量约为 $\sim$1.1 M$_\odot$，而 AGB 恒星返还到星际介质中的物质通常来自质量约为 $\sim$1.2 M$_\odot$、质量损失率约为 $2-3 \times 10^{-6}$ M$_\odot$ yr$^{-1}$ 的恒星。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.10656v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: A method of determining ages and masses of asymptotic giant branch (AGB) stars between 0.8 and $\sim$6 M$_\odot$ is demonstrated, based on comparing the star's position in the period--absolute-magnitude diagram to theoretical evolutionary models. For samples of Milky Way stars, the method provides errors (statistical and systematic, respectively) of order of $^{+29}_{-35} \pm 15$ per cent in age, $^{+14}_{-7} \pm 7$ per cent in initial mass and $^{+17}_{-11} \pm 27$ per cent in current mass. However, its applicability to individual stars depends strongly on both their position in the $P-L$ diagram and the uncertainty of that position. This method is applied to published samples of AGB stars from the \emph{Gaia}, NESS, DEATHSTAR and ATOMIUM surveys. These surveys' statistical ensembles are compared to expectations from stellar evolutionary models, finding that most AGB samples are biased towards stars of younger ages and higher masses. An average mass for Milky Way AGB stars is found to be $\sim$1.1 M$_\odot$, while mass returned to the interstellar medium by AGB stars typically comes from $\sim$1.2 M$_\odot$ stars with mass-loss rates of order $2-3 \times 10^{-6}$ M$_\odot$ yr$^{-1}$.

## Paper 5

**Chinese Title**:  
盖亚天体测量结果不支持长次级周期源于双星系统

**English Title**:  
Gaia astrometry disfavors a binary origin for long secondary periods

**Chinese Abstract**:  
约三分之一的高光度脉动红巨星表现出长次级周期（LSP）：除其更短的主脉动周期外，还存在周期为数月到数年的稳定光度变化。如今距离其发现已近一个世纪，LSP 的物理起源仍未解决。一种主流解释诉诸双星模型，其中 LSP 对应于一个低质量伴星的轨道周期，而该伴星同时导致光度变化和径向速度（RV）调制。我们利用来自《盖亚》Focused Product Release 的邻近 LSP 恒星样本检验这一假设，该数据集提供了多历元 RV 以及同时期的光学测光。我们发现，若将观测到的 RV 变化解释为轨道运动，则推得伴星质量会集中分布在 $M_2 \approx 0.1~{\rm M_\odot}$ 附近，轨道间距为 1--3 au，这恰好落在其太阳型前身星周围观测到的“褐矮星荒漠”范围内。假定这类伴星确实存在，我们进一步正向建模了在《盖亚》DR3 中预期的天体测量信号，并预测邻近 LSP 恒星应系统性地具有偏高的 {\tt RUWE} 值。相反，邻近 LSP 恒星实际观测到的 {\tt RUWE} 系统性低于这些预测，并且与大多数表现出 LSP 的系统实际上是单星这一情形一致。这一差异不利于将低质量恒星或亚恒星伴星视为演化恒星中 LSP 的主导起源，从而促使我们进一步探索其他恒星物理机制。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.09767v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Approximately one-third of luminous pulsating red giant stars exhibit long secondary periods (LSPs): stable photometric variability with periods of several months to years in addition to their much shorter primary pulsation cycles. Now nearly a century after their discovery, the physical origin of LSPs remains unresolved. A leading explanation invokes binarity, in which the LSP corresponds to the orbital period of a low-mass companion responsible for both the photometric variability and the radial-velocity (RV) modulation. We test this hypothesis using a nearby sample of LSP stars from the {\it Gaia} Focused Product Release, which provides multi-epoch RVs and contemporaneous optical photometry. We find that interpreting the observed RV variability as orbital motion implies companion masses narrowly distributed around $M_2 \approx 0.1~{\rm M_\odot}$ with separations of 1--3 au, placing them squarely in the brown dwarf desert observed around their solar-type progenitors. Assuming such companions exist, we then forward-model the astrometric signature expected in {\it Gaia} DR3 and predict systematically elevated {\tt RUWE} values for nearby LSPs. In contrast, the observed {\tt RUWE} of nearby LSP stars is systematically lower than these predictions and consistent with most systems exhibiting LSPs being single. This discrepancy disfavors low-mass stellar or substellar companions as the dominant origin of LSPs in evolved stars, motivating a further exploration of alternative stellar mechanisms.

**Authors**:  
Cheyanne Shariat, Kareem El-Badry, Morgan MacLeod, Emily Leiner
