## Paper 1

**Chinese Title**:  
通过散斑成像寻找难以捉摸的 RR Lyrae 伴星

**English Title**:  
Finding the elusive RR Lyrae companions via speckle imaging

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2603.28684v2 公告类型：替换
摘要：尽管 RR Lyrae 星（RRL）在天体物理学中具有关键作用，但其双星性质至今几乎完全未知，因为目前仅有一颗 RRL 被确认为属于双星系统。寻找 RRL 的伴星之所以困难，是因为它们中的大多数若存在伴星，轨道往往较宽；这是由于近轨道系统很可能发生质量转移，从而破坏恒星形成脉动所需的条件。这类宽轨道使得利用高分辨率成像更容易发现 RRL 的伴星。我们使用 Gemini 望远镜上的散斑干涉仪 Zorro 和 ‘Alopeke 对 81 颗 RRL 进行了观测，达到了这类 8 米级望远镜约 20 mas 的衍射极限，因此在 RRL 周围探索了一个新的参数空间。在这 81 颗 RRL 周围，我们新探测到 10 个伴星，其投影分离范围为 20 AU 到 220 AU。对视场污染的分析表明，所有这些探测到的伴星都极有可能是引力束缚的双星成员。基于这些观测，我们估计 RRL 的双星比例高于 12%，并以 99% 的置信水平排除了双星比例高于 25% 的情况。这些数值显著高于先前接近 1% 的估计，尽管以往结果是通过探索不同参数空间的方法得到的。对于具有薄盘运动学特征的 RRL，我们发现其双星比例明显更低，约为 6%；在观测的 16 颗薄盘 RRL 中，仅有 1 颗拥有伴星。我们还通过测量 RRL 最小光度时的颜色研究了这些伴星的性质，结果表明它们是位于红巨星支较低段和主序上部的恒星；这一方法似乎也可作为寻找和分析双星系统中 RRL 的有效手段。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2603.28684v2 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: Despite their key role in astrophysics, the binary properties of RR Lyrae stars (RRL) remain almost completely unknown since only a single RRL is confirmed as belonging to a binary system. Finding companions to RRL is difficult since most of them will be at wider orbits, given that close orbits will likely ensue mass transfer disrupting the conditions to develop stellar pulsations. These wide orbits open the possibility that RRL companions may be more easily found by high-resolution imaging. We observed 81 RRL with the speckle interferometers Zorro and 'Alopeke at the Gemini telescopes, reaching the diffraction limit of $\sim$20 mas of these 8m-class telescopes, and therefore exploring a new parameter space around RRL. We have detected 10 newly identified companions around these 81 RRL, with projected separations between 20 AU to 220 AU. An analysis of the field contamination shows that all of these detected companions are most likely gravitationally bound binaries. From these observations we can estimate an RRL binary fraction higher than 12%, ruling out a binary fraction higher than 25% at the 99% confidence level. These numbers are significantly more elevated than previous estimations which were close to a binary fraction of only 1%, albeit derived with methods exploring a different parameter space. For RRL with thin disc kinematics, we find that the binary fraction is significantly lower, at around 6%, with a single thin disc RRL having a companion out of the 16 observed. The nature of the companions, found to be stars in the lower red giant branch and upper main sequence, is also studied via the measurement of the minimum light colors of the RRL, which appears as a useful method for the search and analysis of RRL in binary systems.

## Paper 2

**Chinese Title**:  
核心氦燃烧红巨星 \kappa Cyg 的 CHARA 干涉观测与 TESS 星震学研究

**English Title**:  
CHARA Interferometry and TESS Asteroseismology of the Core-Helium Burning Red Giant $\kappa$ Cyg

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.13501v2 公告类型：替换
摘要：我们结合 CHARA 阵列中 PAVO 波束合成器开展的长基线可见光干涉观测，以及来自 TESS 的高精度星震学数据，对次红团星 \kappa Cyg 进行了详细研究。这种双重方法使我们能够对核心氦燃烧阶段的恒星演化模型进行严格检验，而该阶段目前仍是理论上具有显著不确定性的区域。利用 PAVO 干涉数据并直接拟合边缘昏暗强度分布，我们测得其半径为 $R = 8.65\pm0.10 \rm R_\odot$。我们采用 Phoenix 模型大气拟合其光谱能量分布（SED），得到 $L = 44.46 \pm 1.09 \rm L_\odot$ 和 $T_{\rm eff} = 5066^{+47}_{-50} \mathrm{K}$。利用 16 个扇区的 TESS 测光数据，我们在 \kappa Cyg 中探测到了清晰的类太阳振荡。通过将振荡频率与采用预测混合（PM）或指数型超调（OS）的 MESA 模型网格进行比较，我们发现，能够再现这些振荡频率的模型会系统性地高估恒星半径，而包含超调的模型也仅表现出略微更好的结果。同样，这些模型还低估了观测到的偶极模周期间隔（$\Delta\Pi_1$）。通过考察相位偏移（$\epsilon_p$），我们得出结论：模型未能正确描述该恒星的内部结构。我们的结果表明，仅匹配由包层主导的星震学可观测量，并不足以保证模型对恒星核心结构、甚至整体结构的正确刻画；这也凸显出，在核心氦燃烧（CHeB）恒星模型中，亟需改进对对流边界混合的处理。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.13501v2 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: We present a detailed study of the secondary red clump star, $\kappa$ Cyg, by combining long-baseline visible interferometry using the PAVO beam combiner at the CHARA Array with high-precision asteroseismology from TESS. This dual approach allowed for a stringent test of stellar evolutionary models in the core helium-burning phase, which remains a regime of significant theoretical uncertainty. Using the PAVO interferometric data and fitting the limb-darkened intensity profile directly, we measured $R = 8.65\pm0.10 \rm R_\odot$. We fitted the spectral energy distribution (SED) using Phoenix model atmospheres and calculated $L = 44.46 \pm 1.09 \rm L_\odot$ and $T_{\rm eff} = 5066^{+47}_{-50} \mathrm{K}$. Using 16 sectors of TESS photometry, we detected clear solar-like oscillations in $\kappa$ Cyg. Through comparison of oscillation frequencies with MESA grids using either predictive mixing (PM) or exponential overshooting (OS), we found that models reproducing the oscillation frequencies systematically overestimate the stellar radius, with overshooting models performing only marginally better. The same models also under-predict the observed dipole-mode period spacing ($\Delta\Pi_1$). By inspecting the phase offset ($\epsilon_p$), we conclude that models misrepresent the interior structure of the star. Our results demonstrate that matching envelope-dominated asteroseismic observables alone is insufficient to ensure a correct core or even global structure, and highlight the need for improved treatments of convective boundary mixing in the models of core helium-burning (CHeB) stars.
