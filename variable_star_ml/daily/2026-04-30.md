## Paper 1

**Chinese Title**:  
TESS食双星的现象学分类

**English Title**:  
The Phenomenological Classification of TESS Eclipsing Binaries

**Chinese Abstract**:  
食双星是研究恒星参数和演化过程的关键天体物理实验室。在本研究中，我们构建了一个基于机器学习的模型，用于对食双星进行系统性的现象学分类。我们首先从ASAS-SN变星星表中提取食双星，并与TESS目标源进行交叉匹配。随后通过统一的数据处理流程对相应的TESS光变曲线进行处理，最终得到一个高质量训练集，包含9576条食双星光变曲线（2801个EA、1930个EB和4845个EW系统）。我们设计并训练了一个全连接神经网络（FCNN），其在验证集和测试集上的分类准确率分别达到99.23%和99.03%，表现出优异性能。将训练好的神经网络应用于从多个恒星星表汇总得到的总计20196个TESS食双星，并结合人工目视检查后，我们最终获得了13376个EA、2114个EB和4706个EW系统。本研究开发的标准化预处理流程和高性能分类器，为未来光度巡天中海量食双星的快速自动分类提供了可靠工具。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.26398v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Eclipsing binaries are crucial astrophysical laboratories for studying stellar parameters and evolutionary processes. In this study, we constructed a machine-learning-based model for systematic phenomenological classification of eclipsing binaries. We first extracted eclipsing binaries from the ASAS-SN variable star catalog and cross-matched them with TESS targets. The corresponding TESS light curves were processed through a unified pipeline, resulting in a high-quality training set of 9576 eclipsing binary light curves (2801 EA, 1930 EB, and 4845 EW systems). We designed and trained a fully connected neural network (FCNN) that achieved accuracy of 99.23% and 99.03% on the validation and test set respectively, demonstrating excellent performance. Applying the trained neural network to a total of 20196 TESS eclipsing binaries collected from multiple star catalogs and performing manual visual inspection, we finally obtained 13376 EA, 2114 EB, and 4706 EW systems. The standardized preprocessing pipeline and high-performance classifier developed in this study provide a reliable tool for the rapid automated classification of massive numbers of eclipsing binary in future photometric surveys.

Shi-Qi Liu, Kai Li, Xiao-Dian Chen, Li-Heng Wang

## Paper 2

**Chinese Title**:  
短周期双星OGLE-BLG-ELL-006503中缓慢的亮度变化

**English Title**:  
A short-period binary OGLE-BLG-ELL-006503 showing slow variations in brightness

**Chinese Abstract**:  
对OGLE项目28年数据的分析表明，周期为0.404天的双星OGLE BLG ELL-006503（V1231 Sco）除了在I波段表现出约0.07等的潮汐相互作用变化外，还叠加了一个时间尺度为5至10年、振幅为0.15至0.17等的亮度漂移。逐季光变曲线显示，这种长期变化是由大型光球黑子（约为0.3个恒星半径）驱动的，这些黑子表现出相对于潮汐相互作用连线定向排列的倾向。食时刻的O-C残差揭示了轨道周期变化，其特征时间尺度约为2×10^6年，表明存在温和的质量再分布，可能与整体磁场重构有关。Gaia视差和绝对星等估计表明，该系统由类太阳恒星组成。由于缺乏食现象且黑子活动极为强烈，这个双星系统可能由晚型、受潮汐形变的恒星构成，而不是典型的W UMa型系统。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.26710v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Analysis of 28 years of OGLE project data for the 0.404 day period binary OGLE BLG ELL-006503 (V1231 Sco) shows a superposition of tidal-interaction variations of about 0.07 magnitude in the I-band onto a time-scale 5-10 year, 0.15-0.17 magnitude brightness drift. Seasonal light-curves suggest that this long-term variability is driven by large photospheric spots (approx 0.3 stellar radii) that exhibit a tendency to orient themselves relative to the tidal interaction line. The eclipse time O-C residuals reveal an orbital period change characterized by a timescale of approximately 2x10^6 years, suggesting a mild mass redistribution, potentially linked to global magnetic fields reconfiguration. Gaia parallax and absolute magnitude estimates indicate a system consisting of solar-type stars. The lack of eclipses and extreme spot activity suggest the binary may consist of late-type, tidally-deformed stars rather than a typical W UMa-type system.

## Paper 3

**Chinese Title**:  
SRGA J115215.0$-$510656：一颗具有盘风迹象的异常长周期食矮新星

**English Title**:  
SRGA J115215.0$-$510656: an unusual long-period eclipsing dwarf nova with disc wind signatures

**Chinese Abstract**:  
我们基于新的时间分辨光度和光谱观测，并结合长时间基线的凌日系外行星巡天卫星（TESS）数据，对灾变变星SRGA J115215.0$-$510656开展了首次详细的光学研究。TESS光变曲线显示出深而重复出现的食，与高倾角几何构型一致，其轨道周期为0.43567659(9)天。爆发期间的食形态与可能的“内向外”型爆发一致，并支持将该系统归类为U Gem型矮新星。通过结合食相位宽度和椭球调制，我们将系统几何限制在$(q,i)$平面上的一条狭窄区域内，允许的质量比范围为$0.28 \lesssim q \lesssim 0.84$，倾角为$i \simeq 75$-$84^{\circ}$。爆发期间单峰巴耳末线持续存在，同时伴随强He II发射和平坦化的巴耳末递减，这表明辐射可能来自盘风或吸积盘上方垂直延展区域。我们探测到了来自晚型次星（约为K3型）的吸收特征，其对红光学波段通量的贡献约为30%。与主序星预期的比较表明，供体星存在中等程度的膨胀，这与轻微演化的次星相一致。凭借其较长的轨道周期、适中的爆发振幅以及发射线特征，SRGA J115215.0$-$510656似乎是一个罕见且极具吸引力的明亮长周期矮新星实例，其爆发期间的光学性质受到盘风过程的影响。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.26702v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: We present the first detailed optical study of the cataclysmic variable SRGA J115215.0$-$510656, based on new time-resolved photometric and spectroscopic observations complemented by long-baseline Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite (TESS) data. The TESS light curve reveals deep, recurring eclipses consistent with a high-inclination geometry and an orbital period of 0.43567659(9)d. The eclipse morphology during outburst is consistent with a possible 'inside-out' type outburst and supports classification of the system as a U Gem-type dwarf nova. By combining eclipse phase width and ellipsoidal modulation, we constrain the system geometry to a narrow locus in the $(q,i)$ plane, with allowed mass ratios $0.28 \lesssim q \lesssim 0.84$ and inclinations $i$ $\simeq$75$-$84$^{\circ}$. The persistence of single-peaked Balmer lines during outburst, together with strong He II emission and a flattened Balmer decrement, points towards emission arising in a disc wind or vertically extended regions above the disc. Absorption features from a late-type secondary star (approximately K3) are detected, contributing roughly 30 per cent of the red optical flux. Comparison with main-sequence expectations suggests that the donor star is moderately inflated, consistent with a mildly evolved secondary. With its long orbital period, modest outburst amplitude, and emission-line characteristics, SRGA J115215.0$-$510656 appears to be a rare and compelling example of a bright, long-period dwarf nova whose optical properties are influenced by disc-wind processes during outburst.

## Paper 4

**Chinese Title**:  
五颗光度变星的自转特征

**English Title**:  
Rotational characteristics of five photometrically variable stars

**Chinese Abstract**:  
本研究考察了一组热恒星的光度变异性，以检验这种变异是否源于表面温度斑点导致的自转调制。我们将根据投影自转速度确定的频率，与基于TESS光变曲线估计的频率进行了比较。在全部五个目标中，光谱频率都低于光度频率，因此仍保留观测到的亮度变化由自转诱导的可能性。在其中两个目标HT Cet和HD 219487中，光度频率谱显示出更为复杂的变异，较可能是脉动所致。我们的结果表明，在温度高于6500 K的恒星中也可能存在通过自转调制产生的斑点活动，这凸显了将光谱和光度分析结合起来研究变异的重要价值。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.26542v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: This study explores photometric variability in a sample of hot stars in order to test whether variability arises from rotational modulation due to surface temperature spots. Frequencies determined from the projected rotational velocities were compared with frequencies estimated on the basis of TESS light curves. In all five cases, the spectroscopic frequency was lower than the photometric one preserving possibility that the observed brightness variations can be rotationally induced. In two of our targets, HT Cet and HD 219487, the photometric frequency spectra suggest more complex variability, likely pulsations. Our results suggest that spot activity inducing rotational modulation may exist in stars hotter than 6500 K, highlighting the value of combined spectroscopic and photometric analysis of variations.

## Paper 5

**Chinese Title**:  
利用ASAS-SN对中间偏振星光学爆发的系统搜索

**English Title**:  
A Systematic Search for Optical Outbursts in IPs Using ASAS-SN

**Chinese Abstract**:  
灾变变星能够表现出光学通量的快速增强。中间偏振星（IP）是其中一个子类，其强磁场会扰乱吸积盘内区，因此通常被认为拥有被截断的吸积盘，很少发生类似矮新星中由盘不稳定性触发的爆发现象。然而，微新星和磁门控爆发的发现表明，即使没有完全发育的吸积盘，这类事件也可能发生。利用全天超新星自动巡天（ASAS-SN）数据，我们识别出一个此前未被认识到的IP短时标光学爆发群体。初步能量估计表明，其中至少一半的爆发可能与微新星相符，但受限于观测采样频率，我们无法对每个事件进行高置信度分类。因此，这些探测结果应被视为IP中短时爆发存在的证据，而非对微新星的确定性鉴别。我们的结果表明，这类爆发在IP中比以往假设的更常见，其中可能包含相当比例的微新星。模拟显示，如果微新星以规则间隔每年发生一次，那么在10年的观测基线内，最长可漏检多达30%的最短时标爆发。在相同假设下，若所有IP都表现出微新星，我们预期有50%到70%的IP会显示这类爆发，但在我们的样本中只有14%的已知IP表现如此。这一差异表明，并非所有IP都会发生微新星。总体而言，本工作建立了首个关于IP中短时爆发探测的综合样本，为未来研究奠定了基础，并提供了一份可供后续分析的微新星候选系统清单。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.26468v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Cataclysmic variables can show rapid increases in optical flux. Intermediate polars (IPs), a subset with strong magnetic fields that disrupt the inner accretion disc, have been thought to possess truncated discs that rarely undergo the disc-instability outbursts seen in dwarf novae. However, the discovery of micronovae and magnetic-gating bursts suggests that such events may occur even without a fully developed disc. Using data from the All-Sky Automated Survey for Supernovae (ASAS-SN), we identify a previously unrecognised population of short-timescale optical outbursts in IPs. Initial energy estimates indicate that at least half of these bursts may be consistent with micronovae, though limited cadence reduces our ability to classify each event with high confidence. These detections should therefore be regarded as evidence of short outbursts in IPs rather than definitive micronova identifications. Our results show that such bursts are more common in IPs than assumed and may include a substantial fraction of micronovae. Simulations reveal that if micronovae occur once per year at regular intervals, up to 30\% of the shortest bursts could be missed over a 10-year observing baseline. Under the same assumptions, and assuming all IPs display micronovae, we would expect 50--70\% of IPs to show these bursts, yet only 14\% of known IPs in our sample do so. This discrepancy suggests that not all IPs undergo micronovae. Overall, this work establishes the first comprehensive set of short burst detections in IPs, providing a foundation for future investigations and a list of candidate micronova systems for follow-up analysis.

**Authors**:  
Jake Mendelsohn, Simone Scaringi, Martina Veresvarska, Krystian I{\l}kiewicz
