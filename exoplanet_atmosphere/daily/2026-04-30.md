## Paper 1

**Chinese Title**:  
三颗行星的故事：三维云反馈增强温热木星的光谱多样性

**English Title**:  
The tale of the 3 planets: 3D cloud feedback enhances the spectral diversity of warm Jupiters

**Chinese Abstract**:  
尽管行星参数仅有细微差异，JWST 仍显示出温热木星光谱存在很大的多样性。然而，驱动这种多样性的主要因素仍不清楚。我们的目标是识别导致 JWST 观测到的三颗温热木星尺寸系外行星光谱差异的机制：WASP-80b 看起来基本无云，而 WASP-107b 和 WASP-69b 的光谱则都由云主导。我们使用同一框架 ADAM（原名 SPARC/MITgcm）对每颗行星建模，该框架在三维中求解云输运、辐射传输和大气环流之间的相互作用。我们考察了三种凝结物：Na$_2$S、KCl 和 MgSiO$_3$，以及四种粒径（0.1、1、5 和 10 $\mu$m）的作用。较高重力的 WASP-80b 中，云会沉降到比 WASP-107b 更深的大气层中，这自然再现了它们之间的光谱差异。对于 WASP-107b，三种云模型能够重现 NIRCam 观测结果：5 $\mu$m 的 Na$_2$S、1 $\mu$m 的 KCl，以及 5 $\mu$m 的 MgSiO$_3$ 模型。然而，这些模型无法匹配在更短波长 NIRISS 中观测到的散射斜率，也无法解释 MIRI 波段中可能存在的硅酸盐特征，这表明云可能具有多峰分布。我们的模型预测，小型硅酸盐颗粒应当是均匀分布的，因此无法解释 MIRI 波段中两侧边缘光谱之间的差异。最后，将同一模型应用于 WASP-69b 时，并未得到与先前研究所提出的、能够拟合其发射光谱的部分多云昼面解。三维环流、云和辐射传输之间的耦合能够增强温热木星系外行星的光谱多样性，尤其是通过重力引起的云量变化来实现。结合多相位、宽波长覆盖的观测，以及耦合云、环流和辐射传输的模型，是推进我们理解这类新天体的关键。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.26911v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: JWST has shown a large diversity in warm Jupiter spectra, despite only small variations in the planetary parameters. However, the main driver of this diversity remains unclear. We aim to identify the mechanisms responsible for the spectral difference of three warm Jupiter-size exoplanets observed by JWST: whereas WASP-80b appears mostly cloud-free, both WASP-107b and WASP-69b have spectra dominated by clouds. We model each planet using the same framework, ADAM (formerly SPARC/MITgcm), which solves for the interactions among cloud transport, radiative transfer, and atmospheric circulation in 3D. We investigate the role of three condensate species, Na$_2$S, KCl, and MgSiO$_3$, and four particle sizes (0.1, 1, 5, and 10 $\mu$m). Clouds settle deeper in the atmosphere of the higher-gravity planet WASP-80b than in WASP-107b, reproducing their spectral difference naturally. For WASP-107b, three clouds can reproduce the NIRCam observations: 5 $\mu$m Na$_2$S, 1 $\mu$m KCl, and 5 $\mu$m MgSiO$_3$ models. However, these cannot match the scattering slope observed at shorter wavelengths in NIRISS and the possible silicate feature in the MIRI bandpass, suggesting a multi-modal distribution of clouds. Our model predicts that small silicate particles should be homogeneously distributed and thus cannot account for the difference between the two limb spectra in the MIRI bandpass. Finally, applying the same model to WASP-69b does not yield a partially cloudy dayside solution that fits the emission spectra, as proposed in a previous study. Coupling among 3D circulation, clouds, and radiative transfer can enhance the spectral diversity of warm Jupiter exoplanets, particularly through changes in cloudiness with gravity. The combination of multi-phase, wide-wavelength coverage and models that couple clouds, circulation, and radiative transfer is key to advancing our understanding of these new objects.

Line, Sagnick Mukherjee, Kazumasa Ohno, Everett Schlawin, Anastasia Triantafillides, Luis Welbanks, Lindsey S. Wiser

## Paper 2

**Chinese Title**:  
利用 MIRI/MRS 对冷态 COCONUTS-2b 中的氧、氮同位素异构体进行观测

**English Title**:  
Oxygen and nitrogen isotopologues on cold COCONUTS-2b observed with MIRI/MRS

**Chinese Abstract**:  
将气态巨行星的成分与其形成路径联系起来，长期以来一直是系外行星科学的目标。尤其是温度低于约 500 K 的冷气态巨行星，此前一直难以开展详细的大气表征。然而，借助 JWST，我们如今能够获得这类冷世界的高信噪比（S/N）光谱，不仅可以测量其主要微量气体的丰度，甚至还能测量其同位素组成，从而为将其与形成路径关联起来开启新的可能性。在本研究中，我们展示了利用中红外仪器中分辨率光谱仪（MIRI/MRS）获得的 COCONUTS-2b 光谱。它是已知最冷的行星质量伴星之一（$\mathrm{T_{eff}}\approx$480 K，与其 M 矮星宿主的分离约为 $\sim$6400 au）。通过结合 MIRI 数据与档案中的 Gemini/FLAMINGOS-2 数据集，我们旨在刻画其极寒大气的化学组成与物理结构，为揭示 COCONUTS-2b 的形成提供基础。我们首次在 MIRI/MRS 数据集上使用其完整光谱分辨率，并开展大气反演，以挖掘由稀有分子及同位素异构体产生的微弱吸收特征。后者通过留一分析和贝叶斯因子比较来识别。我们在 COCONUTS-2b 的大气中稳健探测到了三种同位素异构体，即 $^{15}$NH$_3$、H$_2^{18}$O 和 H$_2^{17}$O。我们首次在一颗冷伴星中发现了水中氧同位素的清晰证据。该数据集展示了 MIRI/MRS 在表征这类冷行星质量伴星大气的成分及同位素含量方面的能力。未来，受约束的元素比和同位素比将为与宿主恒星丰度进行比较，并最终追踪其形成情景，提供独特途径。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.26850v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Linking the composition of gas giant planets to their formation paths has long been a goal in exoplanet science. Especially, cold gas giants with temperatures below $\sim$500K have been out of reach for detailed atmospheric characterization. With JWST, however, we can reach high signal-to-noise (S/N) spectra for such cool worlds and can can measure not only their main trace gas abundances, but even their isotopic content unlocking new possibilities in linking them to their formation paths. In this study, we present the spectrum of one of the coldest planetary-mass companions COCONUTS-2b ($\mathrm{T_{eff}}\approx$480K, separation of $\sim$6400 au from its M dwarf host star) obtained with the Mid-InfraRed Instrument Medium Resolution Spectrometer (MIRI/MRS). Combining the MIRI and archival Gemini/FLAMINGOS-2 data sets, we aim to characterize the chemical composition and physical structure of its frigid atmosphere, setting the stage to uncover insights on the formation of COCONUTS-2b. For the first time on a MIRI/MRS data set, we use the full spectral resolution of MIRI/MRS and perform atmospheric retrievals to unlock the search for faint absorption features by rare molecules and isotopologues. The latter are identified using a leave-one-out analysis and Bayes factor comparison. We robustly detect three isotopologues, namely $^{15}$NH$_3$, H$_2^{18}$O and H$_2^{17}$O in the atmosphere of COCONUTS-2b. We find the first clear evidence of oxygen isotopes in water in a cold companion. This data set demonstrates the capability of MIRI/MRS to characterize such cold planetary-mass companion's atmospheres with respect to their compositional and isotopic content. In the future, the constrained elemental and isotope ratios provide a unique avenue in comparing with the host star's abundances and eventually in tracing formation scenarios.

## Paper 3

**Chinese Title**:  
光谱分辨率对通过地球历时反射光观测进行生物标志物探测的影响

**English Title**:  
The effect of spectral resolution on biosignature detection via reflected light observations of the Earth through time

**Chinese Abstract**:  
NASA 的宜居世界天文台（HWO）将利用反射光光谱在类地系外行星上寻找生物标志物。一个关键的仪器设计参数是分辨本领，它必须在生物标志物可探测性、曝光时间以及探测器噪声约束之间取得平衡。我们评估了探测和表征关键生物标志气体及宜居性指示物所需的分辨本领，这些分子包括 O$_2$、O$_3$、H$_2$O、CH$_4$、CO$_2$ 和 CO，并针对代表太古宙、元古宙和显生宙地球的大气进行了研究。我们将覆盖光谱分辨率（$\lambda/\Delta{\lambda}$）$R=20$-$5000$ 的解析可探测性计算，与采用 rfast 辐射传输模型和 pyEDITH 曝光时间计算器的大气反演相结合，以实现现实的、随波长变化的噪声建模。在可见光波段（$0.4$-$1.0$ $\mu$m），标称分辨率 $R_{Vis}=140$ 足以在类似显生宙的大气中探测 O$_2$。更高的分辨率在理论上可缩短低 O$_2$ 元古宙大气的曝光时间，但这要求暗电流降低超过 $10\times$，并且可能使 H$_2$O 探测的曝光时间增加约 $\sim 2\times$，从而削弱作为后续生物标志物搜索基础的宜居性核心约束。对于低 O$_2$ 大气，效率更高的途径可能是通过 O$_3$ 进行间接推断，其 Hartley-Huggins 吸收带在 $R_{UV}\sim 7$ 时即可探测。在近红外波段（$1.0$-$1.7$ $\mu$m），需要 $R_{NIR}\geq40$ 才能避免 CO$_2$ 与 CO 之间的简并，否则可能会错误地判定存在丰富的 CO。标称的 $R_{NIR}=70$ 足以表征所有“地球历时”情形。上述结果支持 HWO 当前的基线分辨率选择，并为最终确定光谱仪需求提供了可操作的指导，同时保持寻找系外行星生命所需技术的可行性。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.26925v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: NASA's Habitable Worlds Observatory (HWO) will search for biosignatures on Earth-like exoplanets using reflected light spectroscopy. A critical instrument design parameter is resolving power, which must balance biosignature detectability against exposure time and detector noise constraints. We assess the resolving power needed to detect and characterize key biosignature gases and habitability indicators including O$_2$, O$_3$, H$_2$O, CH$_4$, CO$_2$ and CO across atmospheres representing the Archean, Proterozoic, and Phanerozoic Earth. We combine analytical detectability calculations spanning spectral resolutions ($\lambda/\Delta{\lambda}$) $R=20$-$5000$ with atmospheric retrievals using the rfast radiative transfer model and pyEDITH exposure time calculator for realistic wavelength-dependent noise modeling. In the visible ($0.4$-$1.0$ $\mu$m), the nominal resolution $R_{Vis}=140$ is sufficient for detecting O$_2$ in Phanerozoic-like atmospheres. Higher resolutions could theoretically reduce exposure times for low-O$_2$ Proterozoic atmospheres, but require $>10\times$ reductions in dark current and could increase H$_2$O detection exposure times by $\sim 2\times$, penalizing the foundational habitability constraint that anchors downstream biosignature searches. The most efficient path for low-O$_2$ atmospheres may instead be indirect inference via O$_3$, whose Hartley-Huggins bands are detectable at $R_{UV}\sim 7$. In the near-IR ($1.0$-$1.7$ $\mu$m), $R_{NIR}\geq40$ is necessary to avoid a degeneracy between CO$_2$ and CO that could produce false positive detections of abundant CO. The nominal $R_{NIR}=70$ is sufficient for characterizing all Earth-through-time cases. These results support HWO's current baseline resolution choices and provide actionable guidance for finalizing spectrometer requirements while maintaining technological feasibility for the search for life on exoplanets.

**Authors**:  
Samantha Gilbert-Janizek, Jacob Lustig-Yaeger, Joshua Krissansen-Totton
