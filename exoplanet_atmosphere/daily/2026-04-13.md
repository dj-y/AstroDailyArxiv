## Paper 1

**Chinese Title**:  
NCCR PlanetS：系外行星大气的观测与计算表征

**English Title**:  
NCCR PlanetS: Observational and computational characterization of exoplanet atmospheres

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.09385v1 公告类型：新稿
摘要：本章综述了当前系外行星大气表征在观测与理论方面的研究现状，重点介绍了瑞士国家研究能力中心（NCCR）PlanetS 推动的相关进展。内容涵盖了支配系外行星和棕矮星大气动力学、辐射传输、化学过程以及云形成的基本物理和化学过程。综述讨论了用于模拟这些过程的建模方法，范围从简化的一维模型到完全耦合的三维大气环流模型。文中还介绍了作为从观测数据反演大气性质工具的大气反演框架，强调了经典贝叶斯技术和新兴机器学习方法。与此同时，也考察了利用 HST、JWST 以及地基高分辨率光谱仪开展的观测策略。文章特别强调理论与观测之间的相互作用，以及建模、数据分析和仪器发展的协同推进如何共同加深我们对太阳系外行星大气的理解。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.09385v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: This chapter reviews the current state of observational and theoretical efforts in the characterization of exoplanet atmospheres, with a focus on developments enabled through the Swiss National Centre for Competence in Research (NCCR) PlanetS. It covers the essential physical and chemical processes that govern atmospheric dynamics, radiative transfer, chemistry, and cloud formation in exoplanets and brown dwarfs. The review discusses the modeling approaches used to simulate these processes, ranging from simplified 1D models to fully coupled 3D general circulation models. Atmospheric retrieval frameworks are presented as tools for inferring atmospheric properties from observational data, highlighting both classical Bayesian techniques and emerging machine learning methods. Observational strategies using instruments like HST, JWST, and ground-based high-resolution spectrographs are also examined. Special emphasis is placed on the interplay between theory and observation, and how developments in modeling, data analysis, and instrumentation collectively advance our understanding of planetary atmospheres beyond the Solar System.

## Paper 2

**Chinese Title**:  
温暖富金属次海王星上水氢退混合的一个窗口

**English Title**:  
A window for water-hydrogen demixing on warm metal-rich sub-Neptunes

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2512.01805v3 公告类型：替换
摘要：次海王星是数量最多的一类系外行星，但其整体组成仍知之甚少。近期研究认为，只有像天王星和海王星这样非常寒冷的行星，才可能在其包层中经历挥发物分层。因此，受到高出 10^3 至 10^4 倍恒星辐照的凌星温暖次海王星，此前被认为应具有完全互溶的组成。本文提出了 ATHENAIA，一个内部-大气组成反演框架；我们利用该框架结合辐射-对流大气模型与内部结构模型，评估温暖次海王星发生水-氢退混合的可能性，并以 350 K 的行星 TOI-270 d 作为案例研究。我们发现，在富水环境中，氢与水发生退混合所对应的更高温度，为包层整体金属丰度约为太阳值的约 150 至 700 倍的次海王星打开了退混合窗口，而这一范围与 TOI-270 d 相容。质量更大、温度更低的行星更容易发生退混合，但这一过程仍会广泛影响温暖（约 330 至 450 K）的富金属次海王星。因此，将大气金属丰度与完全互溶包层模型相结合，可能会低估包层的整体金属丰度和质量分数。此外，我们发现，同时考虑富金属大气中增强的温室效应以及对流包层中依赖组成的绝热温度梯度，会扩大次海王星出现熔融地幔条件的组成范围。本研究促使人们重新审视当前将次海王星大气与其内部相联系的范式，并推动建立描述次海王星包层中金属丰度梯度起始形成的演化模型。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2512.01805v3 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: Sub-Neptunes represent the largest exoplanet demographic, yet their bulk compositions remain poorly understood. Recent studies suggested that only very cold planets, such as Uranus and Neptune, could experience stratification of volatiles in their envelopes. Transiting warm sub-Neptunes, with $10^3$ to $10^4$ times more stellar irradiation, were therefore believed to have fully-miscible compositions. Here, we present ATHENAIA, an interior-atmosphere composition inference framework we leverage to assess the potential for water-hydrogen demixing on warm sub-Neptunes and for the 350 K planet TOI-270 d as a case study, using radiative-convective atmosphere models coupled to interior models. We find that the higher temperatures at which hydrogen and water demix in water-rich environments open a window for demixing on sub-Neptunes with bulk envelope metallicities of $\sim 150$ to $700\times$ solar, compatible with TOI-270 d. Demixing is easier to achieve on more massive and colder planets, but still broadly affects warm ($\simeq $330 to 450 K) metal-rich sub-Neptunes. Therefore, combining atmosphere metallicities with models of fully-miscible envelopes may lead to underestimated bulk envelope metallicities and mass fractions. Further, we find that considering the increased greenhouse effect in metal-rich atmospheres in concert with the composition-dependent adiabatic gradient in the convective envelope increases the range of compositions under which molten mantle conditions should be expected on sub-Neptunes. This work encourages a reconsideration of the current paradigm for linking sub-Neptune atmospheres to their interiors and motivates evolutionary modeling describing the onset of metallicity gradients in sub-Neptune envelopes.

Caroline Piaulet-Ghorayeb, Daniel P. Thorngren, Eliza M. -R. Kempton, Justin Lipper, Leslie Rogers, Fernanda Correa Horta, Shi Lin Sun

来自宜居带的光谱特征信号

**English Title**:  
Spectral signatures from the habitable zone

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.08993v1 公告类型：新稿
摘要：本研究阐述了探测邻近恒星宜居带行星光谱特征信号的背景与方法。通过理解当前观测设施的局限性，我们可以更好地认识未来望远镜，并评估它们表征系外行星大气的能力。文中给出了一个信噪比分析示例，针对一颗类似当前地球的行星，考察了作为生物特征信号的氧气，以及作为技术特征信号的（丰度增强的）碘化氢。在乐观估计下，地球很容易被探测到，O2 可在 20 小时内完成表征；但增强的 HI 信号只有在数百小时后才可见，表明该信号过于微弱，难以进行现实可行的约束。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.08993v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: This work describes the context and approach for the detection of spectroscopic signatures from planets in the habitable zone of nearby stars. By understanding the limitations of current observatories, future telescopes can be understood, and their ability to characterize the atmospheres of exoplanets estimated. An example calculation is given for the signal-to-noise analysis for a planet like the current Earth of oxygen as a biosignature, and (an enhanced abundance) of hydrogen iodine as a technosignature. In the optimistic estimate, Earth is easily detected, O2 characterized in 20 hours, but signals from enhance HI are only visible after hundreds of hours, indicating the signals are too weak to realistically constrain.

## Paper 5

**Chinese Title**:  
长周期凌星系外行星：探测与表征的进展

**English Title**:  
Long-period transiting exoplanets: advances in detection and characterization

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.09254v1 公告类型：新稿
摘要：目前已探测到的大多数凌星行星，其轨道都可容纳在水星轨道之内，因此暴露于强烈的恒星辐照和相互作用之下，其性质会被显著改变。相比之下，轨道周期更长、温度更低的行星受此类影响较小，因此能够为其形成与迁移历史提供关键见解。对凌星温暖型和温和型行星进行表征，是系外行星研究拼图中缺失的重要一块。针对空间光度数据中发现的凌星事件开展专门的测光与光谱后续观测，为探测长周期凌星系外行星打开了道路。这些凌星行星拥有丰富的信息，因此是进行深入表征的黄金目标。对于巨行星而言，将精确测得的质量、半径和年龄与最先进的行星演化模型结合，可以估算其整体组成，这对于探索其形成与演化路径至关重要。此外，这些行星也是可能拥有卫星和行星周环的极具吸引力的候选者，而这些特征有助于揭示其动力学历史、卫星形成过程，甚至潜在的宜居环境。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.09254v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Most detected transiting planets have orbits which would fit within the one of Mercury, exposing them to intense stellar irradiation and interactions that significantly alter their properties. In contrast, colder planets with longer orbital periods are less affected, offering crucial insights into their formation and migration histories. Characterizing transiting warm and temperate planets is a key missing piece in the exoplanet puzzle. Dedicated photometric and spectroscopic follow-up of transiting events detected in space-based photometric data opened the way to detecting long-period transiting exoplanets. The wealth of information available for these transiting planets makes them golden targets for in-depth characterization. For giant planets, combining precise masses, radii, and ages with state-of-the-art planetary evolution models allows the estimation of their planetary bulk compositions, a crucial element to explore their formation and evolution pathways. Furthermore, these planets are compelling candidates for hosting moons and circumplanetary rings-features that could illuminate dynamical histories, satellite formation processes, and even potential habitable environments.

Sol\`ene Ulmer-Moll, Babatunde Akinsanmi, Simon M\"uller
