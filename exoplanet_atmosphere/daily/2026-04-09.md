## Paper 1

**Chinese Title**:  
行星大气流体动力学外流透射光谱理论：谱线饱和与质量损失约束的极限

**English Title**:  
A theory of transmission spectroscopy of hydrodynamic outflows from planetary atmospheres: Spectral-line saturation and limits on mass-loss constraints

**Chinese Abstract**:  
摘要：透射光谱是表征系外行星大气的关键技术，已被广泛应用于正在经历流体动力学逃逸的行星。尽管针对静水平衡大气的透射光谱已经存在稳健的解析理论，但对于逃逸大气的相应解释迄今仍主要依赖数值建模，尽管关于行星风的观测数量正在不断增加。在这项工作中，我们通过将标准透射几何与稳态、球对称、等温外流相耦合，发展出一套适用于流体动力学逃逸大气的透射光谱理论。该方法给出了行星风弦向光学深度和有效凌星半径的闭式表达式，并使得光学深度反演问题得以研究。解析解表明，行星风的透射光谱天然分为两个区域。在受不透明度限制的区域中，透射深度仍然对大气质量损失率 dot{M} 保持敏感。然而，一旦超过某个临界阈值，谱线线芯就会达到饱和，不再能对逃逸率提供唯一约束。这一转变由如下形式的尖锐解析边界所标记：sigma(lambda)·dot{M} ≤ C_sat，其中 sigma(lambda) 是谱线吸收截面，C_sat 是由行星风的热力学和几何性质决定的常数。该条件规定了透射深度与质量损失率之间的反演何时存在实数解。一旦该条件被破坏，有效凌星半径将不再由不透明度或质量损失控制，而是由吸收性行星风的几何延展范围决定。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2601.15152v2 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: Transmission spectroscopy is a key technique in the characterization of exoplanet atmospheres and has been widely applied to planets undergoing hydrodynamic escape. While a robust analytic theory exists for transmission spectra of hydrostatic atmospheres, the corresponding interpretation for escaping atmospheres has so far relied on numerical modeling, despite the growing number of observations of planetary winds. In this work, a theory of transmission spectroscopy in hydrodynamically escaping atmospheres is developed by coupling the standard transmission geometry to a steady-state, spherically symmetric, isothermal outflow. This approach yields closed-form expressions for the chord optical depth and effective transit radius of a planetary wind and allows the optical depth inversion problem to be examined.The analytic solution reveals that transmission spectroscopy of planetary winds naturally separates into two regimes. In an opacity-limited regime, transmission depths retain sensitivity to the atmospheric mass-loss rate $\dot{M}$. Beyond a critical threshold, however, spectral-line cores become saturated and no longer provide a unique constraint on the escape rate. This transition is marked by a sharp analytic boundary of the form $\sigma(\lambda)\,\dot{M} \le C_{\rm sat}$, where $\sigma(\lambda)$ is the line absorption cross-section and $C_{\rm sat}$ is a constant set by the thermodynamic and geometric properties of the wind. This condition specifies when the inversion between transmission depth and mass-loss rate admits a real solution. Once it is violated, the effective transit radius is no longer controlled by opacity or mass loss, but by the geometric extent of the absorbing wind.

## Paper 2

**Chinese Title**:  
GEMS JWST：HATS-75 b——一颗环绕 M 矮星运行、具有低于太阳金属丰度大气的巨行星

**English Title**:  
GEMS JWST: HATS-75 b -- A giant planet with a sub-solar metallicity atmosphere orbiting an M-dwarf

**Chinese Abstract**:  
摘要：HATS-75 b 是近期发现的环绕 M 矮星运行的巨型系外行星（GEMS）之一，其透射光谱同时受到自身大气和其凌越的活跃恒星表面的影响。作为一个利用 JWST 研究 7 颗 GEMS 的项目的一部分，我们使用 NIRSpec PRISM 仪器（0.6–5.3 微米）观测了 HATS-75 b 的三次凌星。该行星的光谱在较短波长处表现出略大的凌星深度，这表明存在霾，或者由于凌星轨迹之外恒星表面的非均匀性所导致的恒星污染，即凌星光源（TLS）效应。虽然带霾的大气模型和 TLS 模型都可以复现该透射光谱，但独立证据（例如恒星自转、斑点穿越事件）更支持包含未被遮掩的星斑和光斑污染的模型。在这一基于恒星非均匀性/TLS 的框架下，大气反演给出了极低的大气金属丰度（log[M/H] = -1.74^(+0.92)_(-0.76)）和高于太阳的碳氧比（C/O = 1.04^(+0.40)_(-0.09)）；结合最佳拟合内部结构模型所给出的整体金属丰度 Z_p = 0.20 +/- 0.04，这意味着该行星内部的垂直混合效率较低。反演结果还稳健地探测到 CH4、CO 和 CO2 的吸收特征。对于 H2O，我们仅获得其上限，这与其大气光谱特征被恒星污染掩盖的情形一致。这些结果强调了在解释系外行星透射光谱时考虑恒星表面非均匀性的重要性，并凸显 HATS-75 b 作为利用 JWST 理解环绕 M 矮星运行的巨行星的重要研究对象。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.07268v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: HATS-75 b is one of the recently discovered Giant Exoplanets orbiting M-dwarf Stars (GEMS) with a transmission spectrum shaped by both its atmosphere and the active stellar surface it transits. As part of a JWST program studying 7 GEMS, we observed three transits of HATS-75 b with the NIRSpec PRISM instrument (0.6-5.3 um). The planet's spectra exhibit a slightly larger transit depth at shorter wavelengths, indicative of hazes or stellar contamination due to stellar heterogeneities outside the transit chord, i.e., the transit light source (TLS) effect. While both a hazy atmospheric model or TLS model can replicate the transmission spectrum, independent evidence (.e.g, stellar rotation, spot-crossing events) favors a model that includes contamination from unocculted starspots and faculae. Within this stellar heterogeneity / TLS-based framework, atmospheric retrievals yield remarkably low atmospheric metallicity (log[M/H]=-1.74^{+0.92}_{-0.76}) and super-solar carbon-to-oxygen (C/O=1.04^{+0.40}_{-0.09}), which paired with a best-fit interior model with bulk metallicity of Z_p=0.20+/-0.04, implies poor vertical mixing within the planet. Retrievals also detect robust absorption signatures of CH4, CO, and CO2. We obtain only an upper limit for H2O, consistent with its atmospheric spectral features being masked by stellar contamination. These results underscore the importance of accounting for stellar heterogeneity when interpreting exoplanet transmission spectra and highlight HATS-75 b as a significant asset to our understanding of giant exoplanets around M-dwarfs with JWST.

**Chinese Title**:  
寒冷类木行星系外行星 COCONUTS-2 b 的泛色观测图景

**English Title**:  
Panchromatic View of the Frigid Jovian Exoplanet COCONUTS-2 b

**Chinese Abstract**:  
摘要：我们使用了 COCONUTS-2 b 的一条高信噪比 MIRI-LRS 光谱（5.45–11 微米，R_lambda 约为 100），揭示出显著的 H2O、CH4 和 NH3 分子特征。我们将该数据集与 Gemini/FLAMINGOS-2 和 JWST/NIRSpec（G395H）的光谱，以及来自 WISE 和 Spitzer 的测光数据相结合，从而获得了 1 到 15 微米几乎连续的波长覆盖。我们利用五组自洽大气模型网格对这些数据进行分析，参数范围覆盖广泛的有效温度 T_eff、log(g) 和 [M/H]。我们还研究了使用高斯过程来处理相关噪声的方法，这些噪声可能由光谱仪引起，也可能源于反演框架中模型的系统性偏离。所有模型都能够拟合总体联合观测结果，但在 Y 波段和 N 波段预测出更暗的通量。经典模型比较表明，ATMO2020++ 合成光谱（包括和不包括 PH3）在统计上更受偏好。在对三台光谱仪的相关噪声进行拟合后，ATMO2020++ 模型给出的约束与先前研究和演化模型预测一致：T_eff = 496^(+5)_(-3) K，log(g) = 4.30^(+0.04)_(-0.02) dex，[M/H] = -0.02^(+0.03)_(-0.02) dex，以及 R = 1.03^(+0.01)_(-0.02) R_jup。MIRI 提供的扩展波长覆盖（占总热辐射通量的 41%）补全了该天体的光谱能量分布，从而得到精确的光度估计：log(L/L_sun) = -6.166 ± 0.002 dex。结合先前对系统年龄的估计（414 ± 23 Myr），冷却模型预测其质量为 M = 7.3 ± 0.3 M_jup。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.07176v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: We use a high signal-to-noise MIRI-LRS spectrum (5.45 - 11 $\mu$m, R$_\lambda$ $\sim100$) of COCONUTS-2~b revealing prominent molecular features of H$_2$O, CH$_4$ and NH$_3$. This dataset is combined with spectra from Gemini/FLAMINGOS-2 and JWST/NIRSpec (G395H), as well as photometry from WISE and Spitzer, resulting in almost continuous wavelength coverage from 1 to 15 $\mu$m. We analyze the data using five grids of self-consistent atmospheric models, spanning a wide range of T$_\text{eff}$, log(g), and [M/H]. We also investigate the use of Gaussian Processes to account for correlated noise either caused by the spectrograph or by systematic departures of models in the inversion framework. All models manage to fit the overall combined observations but predict fainter flux in Y- and N-bands. Classical model comparison suggests that the ATMO2020++ synthetic specra (with and without PH$_3$) are statistically preferred. Fitting for the correlated noise of the three spectroscopic instruments, ATMO2020++ models yields constraints consistent with previous studies and evolutionary models predictions: T$_\text{eff}$ $=496^{+5}_{-3}$ K, log(g) $=4.30^{+0.04}_{-0.02}$ dex, [M/H] $=-0.02^{+0.03}_{-0.02}$ dex, and R $=1.03^{+0.01}_{-0.02}$ R$_\text{jup}$. The extended wavelength coverage provided by MIRI (accounting for 41% of the bolometric flux) completes the SED, yielding a precise luminosity estimation of log(L/L$_{\odot}$) $=-6.166\pm0.002$ dex. Combined with a previous estimate of the system age ($414\pm23$ Myr), cooling models predict a mass of M $=7.3\pm0.3$ M$_\text{jup}$.
