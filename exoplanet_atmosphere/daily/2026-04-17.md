## Paper 1

**Chinese Title**:  
CS2 的识别以及暖巨行星大气中碳-硫化学耦合的证据

**English Title**:  
The Identification of CS2 and Evidence for Carbon-Sulfur Chemical Coupling in a Warm Giant Exoplanet Atmosphere

**Chinese Abstract**:  
利用詹姆斯·韦布空间望远镜（JWST）进行的透射光谱观测，正在揭示巨型系外行星大气中日益增长的化学复杂性。其中尤其值得关注的是硫，因为在 JWST 之前，硫几乎没有任何观测约束。近期研究表明，行星大气中的硫含量能够追踪其难熔物质储量，因此是行星形成路径的敏感指示量。然而，尽管 JWST 数据库不断扩大，巨型系外行星的硫元素清单仍然约束较差：含硫物种受非平衡化学以及仍在修订中的动力学网络控制。在这里，我们给出暖巨行星 WASP-80 b 的透射光谱，该数据来自 JWST/NIRCam 和 MIRI 在三次凌星期间对 2.4 $\mu$m--10$\mu$m 波段的观测。我们在其大气中发现了 H$_2$O、CH$_4$、CO$_2$、NH$_3$ 和 CS$_2$ 的证据，并对 CO 和 SO$_2$ 给出了上限。我们的大气反演得到 $\log_{10}\mathrm{X}_{\mathrm{CS_2}} = -2.25^{+0.33}_{-0.32}$。这一 CS$_2$ 丰度明显高于早期针对 WASP-80 b 所处温度范围、富 H$_2$ 大气的硫化学方案所预测的值，但与近期经过化学验证、包含通过 CH$_2$S 实现高效碳-硫耦合的网络相一致。这些结果表明，CS$_2$ 是一个可观测的硫非平衡化学示踪物，并为巨型系外行星大气中理论预测的碳-硫化学耦合提供了观测支持。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.13168v2 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: Transmission spectroscopy with the James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) is revealing growing chemical complexity in giant exoplanet atmospheres. Of particular interest is sulfur, which had essentially no observational constraints before JWST. Recent work has shown that a planet's atmospheric sulfur content traces its refractory budget and is therefore a sensitive indicator of formation pathways. But despite the growing library of JWST data, the sulfur inventory of giant exoplanets remains poorly constrained: sulfur-bearing species are governed by disequilibrium chemistry and by kinetic networks that are still being revised. Here we present a transmission spectrum of the warm giant planet WASP-80 b obtained with JWST/NIRCam and MIRI over 2.4 $\mu$m--10$\mu$m in three transits. We find evidence for H$_2$O, CH$_4$, CO$_2$, NH$_3$, and CS$_2$ in the atmosphere and place upper limits on CO and SO$_2$. Our atmospheric retrievals yield $\log_{10}\mathrm{X}_{\mathrm{CS_2}} = -2.25^{+0.33}_{-0.32}$. This CS$_2$ abundance is substantially higher than predicted by earlier sulfur-chemistry schemes for H$_2$-rich atmospheres in WASP-80 b's temperature range, but is consistent with recent chemically validated networks that include efficient carbon-sulfur coupling through CH$_2$S. These results identify CS$_2$ as an observable tracer of sulfur disequilibrium chemistry and provide observational support for theoretically predicted carbon-sulfur chemical coupling in giant exoplanet atmospheres.

## Paper 2

**Chinese Title**:  
多云且可能富含金属：高分辨率 K 波段光谱显示 GJ 1214 b 大气中存在 CO$_2$ 的迹象

**English Title**:  
Cloudy with a chance of metals: Indications of CO$_2$ in the atmosphere of GJ 1214 b from high-resolution K-band spectroscopy

**Chinese Abstract**:  
亚海王星系外行星常常表现出被削弱的透射光谱，其中 GJ 1214 b 是最突出的例子。经过多年高强度观测却仅得到无明显特征的行星光谱之后，JWST 的最新观测首次揭示了可能的大气信号。我们基于使用 CRIRES$^+$ 光谱仪在 K 波段获得的 8 次凌星观测，对 GJ 1214 b 进行了高分辨率透射光谱研究。我们使用 SYSREM 去除地球大气和恒星信号，并采用互相关技术搜索 H2O、CO、CH4、H2S、NH3 和 CO2 的特征。前五种分子均未检出，我们利用注入-恢复测试对该大气成分给出了上限。对于 CO$_2$，我们测得一个信噪比约为 3.6 的 CCF 信号，详细分析表明没有明显迹象显示该信号由相关噪声造成。Welch t 检验进一步确认，拖迹内与拖迹外的分布在 $3.4 \sigma$ 置信水平上存在差异。采用自由化学组成的贝叶斯反演框架后，得到的体积混合比对应于金属丰度 $[\mathrm{M/H}]=0.48^{+0.89}_{-1.70}$、不透明云层顶压力 $\log_{10}(P_\mathrm{c}) = -3.04^{+2.52}_{-1.53}$，以及行星温度 $T_\mathrm{iso}=398^{+283}_{-197}$ K，这与 JWST 数据推导出的昼夜两侧温压廓线之间的中间值一致。尽管这些参数对应于中红外波段中 CO2 特征相对较大的预期信号幅度，但在模型 $1.5\sigma$ 不确定度范围内，它们与 JWST NIRSpec 观测相容。要确认这些大气信号并对低分辨率与高分辨率数据进行全面解释，还需要进一步建模和更多观测数据。总体而言，我们的结果支持先前的发现，即 CO2 很可能是 GJ 1214 b 大气的重要组成部分。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.15292v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Sub-Neptune exoplanets frequently exhibit muted transmission spectra, with GJ 1214 b being the most prominent example. Following years of intense observing campaigns yielding featureless planetary spectra, recent observations with JWST revealed the first possible atmospheric signatures. We present high-resolution transmission spectroscopy of GJ 1214 b based on eight transits obtained with the CRIRES$^+$ spectrograph in the K band. We used SYSREM to remove telluric and stellar signals and searched for signatures of H2O, CO, CH4, H2S, NH3, and CO2 using the cross-correlation technique. We obtained non-detections for the first five molecules and used injection recovery tests to derive upper limits on the atmosphere. For CO$_2$ we measure a CCF signal at S/N ~ 3.6, with a detailed investigation showing no obvious indication that it is caused by correlated noise. A Welch t-test confirmed the in-trail and out-of-trail distributions to be different at $3.4 \sigma$ confidence. A Bayesian retrieval framework with free chemistry, resulted in volume mixing ratios corresponding to a metallicity of $[\mathrm{M/H}]=0.48^{+0.89}_{-1.70}$, an opacity deck pressure of $\log_{10}(P_\mathrm{c}) = -3.04^{+2.52}_{-1.53}$ and a planet temperature of $T_\mathrm{iso}=398^{+283}_{-197}$ K, consistent with a value intermediate between the day- and night-side T-p's derived from JWST data. While these values correspond to relatively large signal amplitudes predicted for CO2 features in the mid-infrared, they are compatible with JWST NIRSpec observations within the models' $1.5\sigma$ uncertainties. Further modelling and additional data are required to confirm the atmospheric signatures and obtain a comprehensive interpretation of low- and high-resolution data. Overall, our results support previous findings that CO2 is likely to be a significant component of the atmosphere of GJ 1214 b.

**Chinese Title**:  
在岩质系外行星上，大气提供氰化氢并不是前生物化学的速率限制步骤

**English Title**:  
Atmospheric Supply of Hydrogen Cyanide Is Not the Rate-limiting Step for Prebiotic Chemistry across Rocky Exoplanets

**Chinese Abstract**:  
氰化氢（HCN）对“RNA 世界”假说至关重要，因为它能够形成早期生命所必需的生物分子。生命很可能在大约 40 亿年前的太古宙早期出现；当时地球上的太阳更暗、撞击频繁，而且大气仅为弱还原性。温暖小池塘（WLP）是假设中的受保护水环境，有助于解释在如此恶劣环境中脆弱的前生物化学如何出现并演化。WLP 需要经历蒸发和再水化循环，从而浓缩前生物分子，提高（去）聚合发生的可能性，并形成早期 RNA 分子。我们使用一维大气化学模型，将大气向 WLP 输送的 HCN 与外源供给进行比较。以太古宙早期地球为基准，我们考察了大气 HCN 输送对大气 C/O 比、半长轴、假定宿主恒星类型以及甲烷储量的敏感性，从而探索不同岩质系外行星上的条件。我们发现，大气 HCN 输送对这些参数较为敏感，但其数值通常高于陨石输送，也高于我们的太古宙地球基准模型。位于 G 型恒星宜居带内且具有较高 C/O 比的大气，以及紧密环绕 M 矮星运行的行星，可提供最多的大气 HCN。我们发现，大气 HCN 输送具有显著稳健性，因此这种分子很可能不是岩质系外行星上前生物化学出现过程中的速率限制步骤。这一结论尽管附带重要的限制条件，但可能提高生命在其他世界出现的概率。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2603.18769v2 Announce Type: replace
Abstract: Hydrogen cyanide (HCN) is crucial for the RNA World hypothesis, forming biomolecules essential for early life. Life likely emerged around 4 billion years ago during the early Archean Eon, a period on Earth with a fainter sun, frequent impacts, and a weakly reducing atmosphere. Warm little ponds (WLPs) are hypothetical protective aqueous environments that help explain the emergence and evolution of fragile prebiotic chemistry in such a hostile environment. WLPs need to undergo cycles of evaporation and rehydration, concentrating prebiotic molecules that increase the likelihood of (de-)polymerisation and forming early RNA molecules. We use a 1-D model of atmospheric chemistry to compare atmospheric HCN delivery to WLPs with exogenous sources. Using early Archean Earth as our baseline, we examine the sensitivity of atmospheric HCN delivery to the atmospheric C/O ratio, semi-major axis, assumed stellar host type, and methane budget, exploring conditions across rocky exoplanets. We find that atmospheric HCN delivery is sensitive to these parameters but its values generally exceed that of meteoritic delivery and our baseline Archean Earth. Planetary atmospheres with higher C/O ratios within the habitable zones of G stars and those closely orbiting M-dwarfs deliver the most atmospheric HCN. We find that atmospheric HCN delivery is remarkably robust, so this molecule is likely not the rate limiting step for the emergence of prebiotic chemistry on rocky exoplanets. This finding, with important caveats, potentially increases the probability of life emerging on other worlds.

Gergely Friss, Paul I. Palmer, Marrick Braam, Ken Rice
