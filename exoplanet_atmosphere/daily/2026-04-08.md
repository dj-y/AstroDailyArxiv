## Paper 1

**Chinese Title**:  
基于 JWST 和地基 VLT 凌星透射光谱对 WASP-96b 的超太阳金属丰度及光化学过程的初步证据

**English Title**:  
Super-Solar Metallicity and Tentative Evidence for Photochemistry on WASP-96b from JWST and Ground-Based VLT Transmission Spectroscopy

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.05049v1 公告类型：新
摘要：与以往空间天文台相比，JWST 具有更宽的波长覆盖范围和更高的精度，这为重新研究基准行星并以全新视角审视它们提供了机会。本文结合一次新的 JWST NIRSpec/G395H 凌星观测以及档案中的 NIRISS/SOSS 和 VLT/FORS2 透射光谱，对热土星 WASP-96b 的大气进行了深入研究。合并后的光谱清晰显示出 H2O、CO2 和 Na 的特征。然而，CO 仍然缺乏约束，因此仅凭自由反演无法稳健推导其金属丰度。不过，自洽模型网格给出了其大气金属丰度大体上高于恒星金属丰度的结果，为恒星值的 2 至 6 倍。再结合大致与恒星相当的 C/O 比（由自洽网格得到为 0.41^{+0.10}_{-0.09}），我们发现 WASP-96b 可能是在 H2O 雪线之外通过核吸积形成，随后又吸积了富含挥发物的物质。自由反演还显示，包含 SO2 的模型相较于不包含 SO2 的模型有中等程度的偏好（ln B=2.69）。WASP-96b 恰好落在所提出的“SO2 海岸线”上，反演得到的 SO2 丰度也与光化学模型的预测较为一致。我们的合并光谱在光学波段呈现出一个斜率，模型可用散射气溶胶的不透明度来拟合，即小颗粒凝结云或光化学霾；不过，我们不能完全排除 Na 的宽翼结构或恒星污染的影响。未来还需要进一步观测来区分这些效应。最后，我们探讨了 WASP-96b 透射光谱中边缘不对称的可能性，并给出了几种在数据中识别不对称性的检验方法。我们鼓励学界优先发展一条稳健的路径，以量化边缘不对称的存在，尤其是在低信噪比情形下。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.05049v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: With its expanded wavelength coverage and increased precision compared to previous space-based observatories, JWST provides the opportunity to revisit benchmark planets and view them in a new light. Here, we conduct an in-depth study of the atmosphere of the hot-Saturn WASP-96b combining a new JWST NIRSpec/G395H transit with archival NIRISS/SOSS and VLT/FORS2 transmission spectra. The combined spectrum shows clearly-visible features from H2O, CO2, and Na. CO, though, remains unconstrained, precluding a firm metallicity derivation from free retrievals alone. However, self-consistent grids yield a broadly super-stellar atmospheric metallicity of 2-6x stellar. When combined with a roughly stellar C/O ratio ($0.41^{+0.10}_{-0.09}$ from self-consistent grids), we find that WASP-96b potentially formed via core-accretion beyond the H2O snowline and subsequently accreted volatile-rich material. Free retrievals also find a moderate preference (ln B=2.69) for models with SO2 versus without. WASP-96b falls directly on the proposed "SO2 shoreline" and the retrieved SO2 abundance is well-matched to predictions from photochemical models. Our combined spectrum displays an optical slope, which our models fit with opacity from scattering aerosols -- either small-particle condensate clouds or photochemical hazes -- though we cannot completely rule out the broad wings of Na or the effects of stellar contamination. Future observations are necessary to disentangle these effects. Finally, we explore the possibility for limb asymmetry in WASP-96b's transmission spectrum and provide several tests to identify asymmetries in our data. We encourage the community to prioritize the development of a robust pathway to quantify the presence of limb asymmetry -- particularly for low signal-to-noise cases.

基于十年多波段凌星后续观测的热木星 WASP-43 b 的凌星计时与透射光谱研究

**English Title**:  
The Transit Timing and Transmission Spectrum of Hot Jupiter WASP-43 b from a decade of Multi-band Transit Follow-up Observations

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.05907v1 公告类型：新
摘要：我们给出了通过 SPEARNET 网络获得的一组新的 35 条热木星 WASP-43 b 的凌星光变曲线。我们将这些数据与此前发表的地基观测以及来自 \emph{TESS}、\emph{HST} 和 \emph{JWST} 的空间观测数据联合分析，以修正 WASP-43 b 的行星参数。我们利用 \texttt{TransitFit} 测得的总计 188 个凌星中心时刻，对可能存在的计时变化进行了分析。凌星时间变化并未显示出轨道衰减的显著证据。利用 \emph{HST}/WFC3 G141 透射光谱进行的大气反演表明，较高温度的解通常对应较高的水丰度。然而，当这些数据与地基望远镜、\emph{TESS} 和 \emph{JWST} 的观测联合使用时，跨越宽波长基线所带来的模型复杂性显著增加，对大气表征提出了重大挑战。这些结果表明，未来需要高精度、多仪器的数据集，才能打破该目标大气建模中现有的简并性。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.05907v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: We present a new set of 35 transit light curves of the hot Jupiter WASP-43~b, obtained through the SPEARNET network. These datasets were analyzed together with previously published ground-based observations, as well as space-based data from \emph{TESS}, \emph{HST}, and \emph{JWST}, to refine the planetary parameters of WASP-43~b. A total of 188 mid-transit times, measured with \texttt{TransitFit}, were analyzed for potential timing variations. The transit timing variations do not show any significant evidence of orbital decay. Atmospheric retrievals using \emph{HST}/WFC3 G141 transmission spectra suggest that higher-temperature solutions are associated with higher water abundances. However, when these data are combined with observations from ground-based telescopes, \emph{TESS}, and \emph{JWST}, the increased modeling complexity across the broad wavelength baseline presents significant challenges for atmospheric characterization. These results highlight that high-precision, multi-instrument datasets will be necessary to break existing degeneracies in the atmospheric modeling of this target in the future.

Investigation of Transit Timing and an Optical Transmission Spectrum of the Hot Jupiter WASP-11 b

**Chinese Abstract**:

摘要：WASP-11 b/HAT-P-10 b 是一颗膨胀的热木星，其低密度使其成为利用透射光谱技术开展大气研究的理想目标。在这项工作中，我们给出了通过 SPEARNET 网络获得的 31 条新的 WASP-11 b/HAT-P-10 b 凌星光变曲线。我们将这些数据与此前发表的地基观测以及来自 \texttt{TESS} 的空间观测数据一并分析。我们重新修正了 WASP-11 b/HAT-P-10 b 的行星参数，并利用跨越 16 年的数据进行了凌星计时分析。更新后的 (O-C) 图未显示出轨道衰减的显著证据。凌星时间变化（TTV）分析也未发现指示额外行星存在的显著信号。基于多波段光学观测的大气分析表明，其透射光谱中存在明显的瑞利散射斜率，这可能源自行星大气本身，也可能受到恒星活动或伴星光等污染因素的影响。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.05570v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: WASP-11~b/HAT-P-10~b is an inflated hot Jupiter, which has a low density that makes it a good target for atmospheric studies using the transmission spectroscopy technique. In this work, we present 31 new transit light curves of WASP-11~b/HAT-P-10~b, obtained through the SPEARNET network. These data were analyzed along with previously published ground-based observations and space-based data from \texttt{TESS}. We refine the planetary parameters of WASP-11~b/HAT-P-10~b and perform a transit timing analysis using data spanning 16 years. The updated ($O-C$) diagram shows no significant evidence of orbital decay. The TTV analysis reveals no significant signals indicative of additional planets. Atmospheric analysis using multi-band optical observations indicates a strong Rayleigh scattering slope in the transmission spectra, which may originate from the planetary atmosphere itself or be influenced by contamination such as stellar activity or light from the companion star.

## Paper 4

**Chinese Title**:  
由引力不稳定性形成的气态巨行星可能吸积具有超恒星碳氧比的大气

**English Title**:  
Gas Giants Formed by Gravitational Instability May Accrete Atmospheres with Super-Stellar Carbon to Oxygen Ratios

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.05178v1 公告类型：新
摘要：表征系外行星大气成分，并结合其质量、平均密度和轨道构型等性质加以判断，被认为是区分不同形成与演化情景的有效手段。在核吸积机制框架下，将系外行星大气的 C/O 比与其宿主恒星的 C/O 比进行比较，已被提出作为区分气态巨行星形成和演化情景的判据。另一方面，通过气体盘引力不稳定性（GDGI）形成的气态巨行星，通常被认为其大气 C/O 比与宿主恒星完全相同。我们利用在通量限制扩散近似下的 GDGI 三维辐射流体力学模型，对这一假设进行了检验。本文表明，GDGI 原行星有可能形成并吸积具有超恒星 C/O 比的盘气体，其原因在于它们形成并发生轨道演化时所处的盘中面温度位于水冰（约 135 K）和 CO2 冰（约 47 K）的蒸发温度范围之间。若固体颗粒能够避免碎裂并快速增长到厘米尺度，它们向内输运至中心原恒星或向外输运至盘边缘的速度可能显著快于盘气体耗散的速度，从而导致富碳盘气体相对于富氧冰更容易被优先吸积，前提是原行星轨道保持在距离一颗太阳质量原恒星约 7 au 之外。然而，若轨道位于约 7 au 以内，则可能吸积到 C/O 比接近恒星值的盘气体。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.05178v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Characterizing the atmospheric compositions of exoplanets, along with determining properties such as their mass, mean density, and orbital configuration, is thought to be an effective means for differentiating between various formation and evolution scenarios. Exoplanet atmospheric C/O ratios, when compared to host star C/O ratios, have been advanced as discriminators of gas giant formation and evolution scenarios in the context of the core accretion mechanism. Gas giants formed by gas disk gravitational instability (GDGI), on the other hand, are thought to have atmospheres with C/O ratios identical to their host stars. We examine this assumption through analysis of fully three dimensional radiative hydrodynamics models of the GDGI in the flux-limited diffusion approximation. We show here that GDGI protoplanets may be able to form and accrete disk gas with super-stellar C/O ratios, as a result of their formation and orbital evolution in a disk with midplane temperatures in the range of the evaporation temperatures of water ($\sim$ 135 K) and CO$_2$ ($\sim$ 47 K) ices. Solids that avoid fragmentation and grow rapidly to cm-size could be transported inward to the central protostar or outward to the edge of the disk considerably faster than the disk gas is dissipated, leading to the preferential accretion of C-rich disk gas compared to the O-rich ices, provided that the protoplanet's orbit remains outside $\sim$ 7 au from a solar-mass protostar. Orbits inside $\sim$ 7 au, however, could result in the accretion of disk gas with nearly stellar C/O.

## Paper 5

**Chinese Title**:  
光蒸发系外行星中的恒星-行星磁相互作用

**English Title**:  
Star-planet magnetic interactions in photoevaporating exoplanets

**Chinese Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.06064v1 公告类型：新
摘要：在近轨系外行星凌星相位附近观测到的周期性恒星活动，为恒星-行星磁相互作用（SPMI）提供了证据，这种相互作用类似于木星与其卫星之间的磁耦合。将 SPMI 信号所对应的功率与解析理论进行比较，为约束系外行星磁场提供了一种方法，但基于卫星-磁层类比的模型往往低估了观测到的能流。与卫星不同，许多近轨系外行星是受到强烈辐照并经历显著光蒸发的延展型气态巨行星。然而，大气逃逸如何影响恒星-行星磁耦合目前尚不清楚。本文给出了三维辐射磁流体力学模拟，在一颗嵌入磁化恒星风中的热木星上，同时模拟行星蒸发和 SPMI。我们的模拟揭示了被称为阿尔芬翼的磁结构的形成，它们将磁能从行星输运出去。当行星白昼侧的质量损失率 \dot{M}_d 低于由行星风与恒星风压强平衡所定义的阈值 \dot{M}_0（即 \dot{M}_d <= \dot{M}_0）时，输送到恒星的最大功率与阿尔芬翼模型的预测一致。对于更高的逃逸率，行星外流会打开额外的磁通量，而 SPMI 功率将按 (\dot{M}_d / \dot{M}_0)^{1/2} 的比例增加。将这一标度关系应用于 HD18973 系统时，我们发现若 \dot{M}_d \sim 10^{12} g/s，则一颗磁场强度为 30 G 的行星可以再现实测功率。尽管这一信号很可能只代表总功率的一部分，但其他机制可能会放大能量预算。这些结果表明，处于亚阿尔芬轨道上的光蒸发系外行星是开展 SPMI 观测的有前景目标。

**English Abstract**:  
arXiv:2604.06064v1 Announce Type: new
Abstract: Observations of periodic stellar activity near the transit phase of a close-in exoplanet provide evidence of star-planet magnetic interactions (SPMI), similar to the magnetic coupling between Jupiter and its moons. Comparing the power associated with SPMI signals to analytical theories offers a way to constrain exoplanetary magnetic fields, but models based on moon-magnetosphere analogs often underpredict observed energy fluxes. Unlike moons, many close-in exoplanets are extended, highly irradiated gas giants undergoing significant photoevaporation. However, it is not known how atmospheric escape influences the star-planet magnetic coupling. Here, we present three-dimensional radiation magneto-hydrodynamic simulations that simultaneously model planetary evaporation and SPMI in a hot Jupiter planet embedded in a magnetised stellar wind. Our simulations reveal the formation of magnetic structures known as Alfv\'en wings, which transport magnetic energy away from the planet. When the dayside mass-loss rate $\dot{M}_d$ of the planet lies below a threshold $\dot{M}_0$ defined by pressure balance between the planetary and stellar winds ($\dot{M}_d \leq \dot{M}_0$), the maximal power delivered to the star matches predictions from the Alfv\'en wing model. For higher escape rates, the planetary outflow opens additional magnetic flux, and the SPMI power increases proportionally with $(\dot{M}_d / \dot{M}_0)^{1/2}$. Applying this scaling law to the HD18973 system, we find that a $30$ G planet could reproduce the observed power if $\dot{M}_d \sim 10^{12}$ g/s. Although this signal likely represents only a fraction of the total power, additional mechanisms could amplify the energy budget. These results show that photoevaporating exoplanets in sub-Alfv\'enic orbits constitute promising targets for SPMI observations.

Andr\'es Presa, Aline A. Vidotto, Filip Elekes
